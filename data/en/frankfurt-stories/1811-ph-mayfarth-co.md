# Ph. Mayfarth & Co.

*Hanauer Landstraße 169–171*

Frankfurt and its colonial history

The establishment of German colonies in Africa and Oceania also promised profitable sales markets for the then expanding agricultural machinery manufacturer Ph. Mayfarth & Co. The company placed adverts in newspapers from the colonies and joined the Colonial Economic Committee, which, among other things, financed the exploration of agricultural potential in the German colonial empire. At the first German colonial exhibition, organised from May to October 1896 in Berlin, Mayfarth presented machines and agricultural implements that were particularly suitable for use in the colonies, according to the advertising claims. The extent of the company's colonial business has not yet been analysed in detail.
