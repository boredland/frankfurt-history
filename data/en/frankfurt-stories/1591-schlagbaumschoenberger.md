# Schlagbaum/Schönberger

*Berger Straße 233*

125 years of FSV (1899-2024)

![](../../images/2f43fc804b15145142195852df784391_original.jpg)

You are now standing in front of the pub ‘Zum alten Schlagbaum’ at Berger Straße 248. There used to be a barrier here as an entrance and customs border to the municipality of Bornheim and there was already an inn here in the 17th century. If you look up towards Berger Straße, you will see the ‘Schöneberger’ pub on the left. The ‘Neue Schlagbaum’ was built there in 1897. The old stone relief with the inscription ‘Zum Schlagbaum’ can still be seen above the entrance door.

From the 1920s until 1953, numerous events for club members were held there, such as Christmas parties, players' meetings and, from 1937, the annual general meetings. When the main stand, which had been destroyed in the war, was rebuilt in 1953, events were moved to the newly opened club restaurant at the stadium.
