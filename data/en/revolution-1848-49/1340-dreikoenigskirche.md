# Dreikönigskirche

*Dreikönigsstraße 32*

![Photo of the "Dreikönigskirche" (Church of the Three Kings)](../../images/0f3678a77f4701784ae54c2aa0ed34bd_original.jpg)
*ca. 1890 — Author: Gottfried Vömel | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Voe Nr. 275 | Description: Photo of the "Dreikönigskirche" (Church of the Three Kings)*

In late summer 1848, several meetings were held in the Church of the Three Kings in Sachsenhausen. These were seeking agreement on political submissions on various issues to the National Assembly – including the Truce of Malmö signed by Prussia and Denmark. The debates were not always conducted in an orderly manner, and some participants even smoked in the church.

*Author: Markus Häfner | License: ISG*
