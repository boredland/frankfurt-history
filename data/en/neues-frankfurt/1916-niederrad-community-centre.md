# Niederrad community centre

*Breubergstrase 6a*

![The Niederrad community centre viewed from the interior courtyard.](../../images/0af7d2b70406d076e0e135a102c46e28_original.jpg)
*aorund 1928 — Author: Hermann Collischonn | License: ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V. | Description: The Niederrad community centre viewed from the interior courtyard.*

The community centre of the Niederrad housing estate is somewhat hidden inside the zigzag block of flats. It was primarily a house for children, as it housed a kindergarten, a nursery, and a children’s library. While the little ones played and were looked after there, the mothers could do the family’s laundry in the basement of the building in the communal laundry, which was equipped with state-of-the-art machines. The central heating system for the entire block was also located in the basement. The building still houses a childcare centre today.

Construction management: Aktienbaugesellschaft für kleine Wohnungen

Construction period: 1926

Architect: Carl-Hermann Rudloff

Further reading: Ernst May: Das soziale Moment in der neuen Baukunst. In: Das Neue Frankfurt, 1928, issue 5.

*Author: Christina Treutlein*
