# Residential neighbourhood at Marbachweg

*Marbachweg 279-293*

![Multi-dwelling units at Marbachweg, around 1930](../../images/6d667af22a992b8e490cd04a6a99193c_original.jpg)
*around 1930 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Das Neue Frankfurt, 1930, Heft 4/5, S. 96 (Sammlung der ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V.) | Description: Multi-dwelling units at Marbachweg, around 1930*

With pictures of this modern block of houses, the construction company Appel & Zahn advertised itself in the magazine “Das Neue Frankfurt”. To this day, the clinker brick elements in particular have remained a striking feature. The clinkered vertical wall projections stand next to the entrance area, which is also clinkered, and structure the smoothly plastered facades of the low-rise buildings with their three to four storeys. The 251 flats are all equipped with central heating and have a kitchen, bathroom, and one to four rooms. Modelled on these houses, further blocks were built in a similar design at Marbachweg 267-269 and Eschersheimer Landstraße.

Construction management: Rhein-Mainische Gesellschaft für Wohnungs- und Siedlungsbau

Construction period: 1927–1929

Architect: Karl Olsson

Further reading: 

Heike Risse: Frühe Moderne in Frankfurt am Main 1920–1933. Frankfurt am Main 1984, p. 246.
