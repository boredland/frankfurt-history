# Forum Neues Frankfurt

*Hadrianstraße 5*

![Forum Neues Frankfurt – Meeting point for information and discussions](../../images/d91e18ca7bb9ff6b6b98435178970c23_original.jpg)
*2018 — Author: Peter Paul Schepp | License: ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V. | Description: Forum Neues Frankfurt – Meeting point for information and discussions*

The Forum Neues Frankfurt is a project of the ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V. and the City of Frankfurt am Main. Here you will find information on all aspects of Neues Frankfurt, as well as a small museum shop, and you can participate in various events. The Forum Neues Frankfurt also houses both the publicly accessible library and the office of the ernst-may-gesellschaft.
The building block with shops on the ground floor and flats on the upper floors was the supply centre of the Römerstadt housing estate. In the shops, residents could buy everything they needed for everyday life.

Construction management: Aktienbaugesellschaft für kleine Wohnungen

Construction period: around 1928

Architect: Carl-Hermann Rudloff

*Author: Christina Treutlein*
