# Martin Elsaesser Foundation

*Herxheimerstraße 31*

![View of the Martin Elsaesser Foundation office in Frankfurt am Main](../../images/211b60bc34b1c6ca4becd6769b57e5f6_original.jpg)
*2019 — Author: Konrad Elsässer | License: Martin-Elsaesser-Stiftung | Description: View of the Martin Elsaesser Foundation office in Frankfurt am Main*

The Martin Elsaesser Foundation was established in 2009. Its aim is to document and make accessible the architectural and artistic legacy as well as the life of architect Martin Elsaesser (1884–1957). For this purpose, an archive and a catalogue raisonné have been created. The foundation organises exhibitions and the editing of various publications, in particular booklets on individual buildings. The office is located in Frankfurt am Main and offers guided tours of the “Elsaesser Exhibition” in the former wholesale market hall (Großmarkthalle, now European Central Bank) and in the renovated Gustav Adolf church. The foundation coordinates and supports various initiatives related to Martin Elsaesser and Neues Bauen.
