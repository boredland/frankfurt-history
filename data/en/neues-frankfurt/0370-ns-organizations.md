# NS organizations

*Hasengasse 2*

From early 1927, the mezzanine floor of Trierische Gasse 7 (now Hasengasse 2) was occupied by the Gauleitung of the NSDAP for Hesse-Nassau South and the local branch of the NSDAP Greater Frankfurt.

Here, the landlord allowed a publicly visible sign with the inscription "National Socialist German Workers' Party of Germany" for the first time.
The rented room also served as a consultation and consultation room for the NSDAP city councillors. On the windows, the Nazis placed the advertisement "Verlag Frankfurter Beobachter", which also had its office there.
