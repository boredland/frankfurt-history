# Goethe University boathouse

*Schaumainkai 70*

![The boathouse at the Main river during the flood of November 1930.](../../images/6e7de32b7a0e38feb5065255b50c4d95_original.jpg)
*1930 — Author: Unbekannr | License: ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V. | Description: The boathouse at the Main river during the flood of November 1930.*

Although it is very centrally located at the Main river, the university boathouse designed by Martin Elsaesser is largely unknown. It was constructed in cooperation with Wilhelm Schütte in 1930 as a functional flat-roofed building with ribbon windows. Due to being located directly next to the river, the building was at risk of flooding from the outset. Thus, gates can be opened on its narrow sides in the case of emergency to allow the river to simply flow through the building. There is a small flat, once intended for the caretaker, on the first floor, as well as changing rooms, showers, and a terrace. Today, the boathouse is used by the institute of sports science, the centre for university sports, and the Germania rowing club.

Construction management: Frankfurt University

Construction period: 1930

Architects: Martin Elsaesser, Wilhelm Schütte

Further reading: Konrad Elsässer, Das Bootshaus der Universität – Am Wasserweg ost-westlich, in: maybrief 55, Frankfurt 2021, p. 30-31.
