# Historical Museum Frankfurt

*Saalhof 1*

![Historical Museum Frankfurt, exterior view](../../images/f1eb5fe0033e50a4d4b2b112fdc9c893_original.jpg)
*2024 — Author: Jan Hassenpflug | License: Historisches Museum Frankfurt | Description: Historical Museum Frankfurt, exterior view*

The Historical Museum Frankfurt (HMF) is the modern city museum of Frankfurt and one of the largest city museums in Europe. It combines 800 years of architectural history in five buildings. Eight exhibitions on 6,000 square metres present topics from the past, present, and future of Frankfurt.

The HMF is a place of knowledge, critical information, reflection, and discussion.

It has received the “Travel for All” label for accessibility. With its Young Museum, it offers special programmes for families and schools. The HMF draws on the wealth of experience and knowledge of the people of Frankfurt and their guests and invites them to participate in the museum and help shape it. One of the locations of the HMF is the Porcelain Museum Frankfurt in the Kronberger Haus in Höchst.
