# Museum Angewandte Kunst

*Schaumainkai 17*

![View of Museum Angewandte Kunst](../../images/51b955addb45613514a6e9eccca5aa50_original.jpg)
*2022 — Author: Günzel/Rademacher, Museum Angewandte Kunst | License: Museum Angewandte Kunst | Description: View of Museum Angewandte Kunst*

The Museum Angewandte Kunst is one of the most internationally recognised and distinguished museums of its kind. It is dedicated to the applied arts, handicrafts, design, fashion, book art, graphics, and architecture, to lifestyles and the performative. As a lively place of discovery, the museum is focused on the perception of social trends and developments. Against the backdrop of its important collections, it aims to make the hidden visible and to create relationships between the events and stories surrounding things. The changing exhibitions tell of cultural values and changing living conditions. In addition, they always refer to the question of what applied art is and can be today.
