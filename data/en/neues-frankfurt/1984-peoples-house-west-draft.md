# People’s house West - draft

*Frankenallee / Ecke Sodener Straße*

![People’s house West, draft](../../images/250281d647b49af4a240b5330f9c212e_original.jpg)
*1930 — Author: Max Cetto | License: Das Neue Frankfurt, 1931, Heft 8, S. 152 (Sammlung der ernst-may-gesellschaft e.V.) | Description: People’s house West, draft*

In August 1930, architect Max Cetto announced the imminent start of construction of the people’s house West in the industrialised Gallus district. An educational and cultural centre was to be built opposite the centrally located Schneidhainer Straße and the newly constructed rows of houses in the Hellerhof housing estate. Cetto had designed a multi-storey building with a hall and stage including cinema facilities for almost 800 visitors, a large public library, a children’s reading room, as well as rooms for exhibitions and educational work. A restaurant was also part of the programme. However, the extremely poor economic situation presumably prevented the realisation of the advanced plans.

Construction management: Volksbildungsheim GmbH

Draft: 1930

Architect: Max Cetto

Further reading: Das Neue Frankfurt, vol. 5, 1931, issue 8, p. 152-153.

*Author: Ulrike May*
