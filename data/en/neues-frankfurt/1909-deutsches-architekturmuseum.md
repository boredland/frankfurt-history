# Deutsches Architekturmuseum

*Schaumainkai 43*

![Interior view of Deutsches Architekturmuseum, 3rd floor](../../images/35a0a0035709a8d54808822ad722186f_original.jpg)
*2024 — Author: Moritz Bernoully | Description: Interior view of Deutsches Architekturmuseum, 3rd floor*

As an exhibition hall and discussion centre, the Deutsches Architekturmuseum (DAM) has been dedicated to national and international topics of architecture and urban planning since 1984 with changing exhibitions, events, and an educational programme. The museum has an extensive international collection of architectural objects, particularly from the 20th and 21st centuries.

In the permanent exhibition, you can see the “Bruchfeldstraße housing estate” from 1927 as one of eleven dioramas – three-dimensional images with buildings and figures illustrate the construction history of the housing estate. Bruchfeldstraße was one of the first major projects in the Neues Frankfurt programme to create affordable housing. The exhibition also includes a model of the Frankfurt kitchen – the first fitted kitchen on a compact floor space.

*Author: Deutsches Architekturmuseum*
