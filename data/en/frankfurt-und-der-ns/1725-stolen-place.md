# Stolen Place

*Mainzer Landstraße 53-57*

![Mainzer Landstraße 53-57](../../images/df60697939d56173e9103ba8c299dc87_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Mainzer Landstraße 53-57*

In 1942, the city acquired the property of the Neues Theater Mainzer Landstraße 53-57 from Theaterbaugesellschaft AG for the price of 355,000 Reichsmark. The theater, which until then had been privately run by Arthur Hellmer, had already been incorporated into the Städtische Bühnen in 1936 by Hans Meisssner, General Director of the Städtische Bühnen, under the name "Kleines Haus". Arthur Hellmer was of Jewish origin; the takeover was considered "Aryanization".
