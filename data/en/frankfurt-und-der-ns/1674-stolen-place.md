# Stolen Place

*Hanauer Landstraße 435*

![Hanauer Landstraße 435](../../images/870133517a87fe4ddddc4eac251b38bd_original.jpg)
*1991 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Hanauer Landstraße 435*

The property at Hanauer Landstraße 435 had originally belonged to the company Cosmo Montan Gesellschaft für Metallurgische Interessen mbH, which had Jewish owners. The anti-Semitic persecutees had to sell their property in 1939 to the company Nica Nickelindustrie August Klüser, which was considered a non-Jewish company.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
