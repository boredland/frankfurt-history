# Stolen Place

*Taunusanlage 12*

![Taunusanlage 12](../../images/d662c2f19d71b13a0916b1718d9c82bc_original.jpg)
*ca. 1949 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Taunusanlage 12*

The property at Taunusanlage 12 originally belonged to Lili Jeanette Schey von Koromla, née von Goldschmidt-Rothschild. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her property to Volksfürsorge Lebensversicherungs-AG of the German Labor Front (DAF) in 1938. The Nazi company was of course considered a non-Jewish company.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
