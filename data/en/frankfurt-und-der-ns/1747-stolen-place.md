# Stolen Place

*Rat-Beil-Straße 10*

![Rat-Beil-Straße 10](../../images/8c503973b36190c792e3b24fafa786d6_original.jpg)
*ca. 1963 — Author: Hermann Nöller | License: HMF | Description: Rat-Beil-Straße 10*

The Old Jewish Cemetery on Rat-Beil-Straße, which had not been occupied with new graves since 1929, had to be forcibly sold to the City of Frankfurt in 1939 as part of the "Jewish Treaties". Many gravesites were stripped of their metal inscriptions and donated to the "metal donation campaign".

Research: Jutta Zwilling
