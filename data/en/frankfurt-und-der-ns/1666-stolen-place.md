# Stolen Place

*Grüne Straße 42*

![Grüne Straße 42](../../images/81f73faea4cf08037319cfe12cacf222_original.jpg)
*2014 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Grüne Straße 42*

The property at Grüne Straße 42 had originally belonged to Lina Guntersheim, née Mosbacher, and her children Emanuel and Johanna. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939. It later served as a "Jewish or ghetto house", in which anti-Semitic persecutees were forcibly concentrated before being deported.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
