# Stolen Place

*Hynspergstraße 27*

![Hynspergstraße 27](../../images/79c6fca8705aebf42174631de0fe676d_original.jpg)
*1938 — Author: Jutta Hofmann | License: ISG | Description: Hynspergstraße 27*

The property at Hynspergstraße 27 had originally belonged to Dina Schmidt, née Guckenheimer. She was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
