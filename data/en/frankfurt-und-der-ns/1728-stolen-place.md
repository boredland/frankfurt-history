# Stolen Place

*Mauerweg 34*

![Mauerweg 34](../../images/f56847dabff57b7f91e9103037250a57_original.jpg)
*1896 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Mauerweg 34*

The property at Mauerweg 34 had originally belonged to Siegfried Loewenthal and his wife Henriette, née Feuchtwanger. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
