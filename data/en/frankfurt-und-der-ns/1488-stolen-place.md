# Stolen Place

*Günthersburgallee 79*

![](../../images/41d52f1e035c6d9221dd69a9ca6662ee_original.jpg)
*License: Hermann Tertilt*

The property at Günthersburgallee 79 originally belonged to Adolf Guckenheimer and his wife Settchen, née Hochstädter. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938. Hermann Tertilt, since 1998 a tenant in an apartment of the house, researched the history of the former owner family in 2022/23 and established contact with the family's descendants.
