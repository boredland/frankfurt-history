# Stolen Place

*Schloßstraße 4*

![Schloßstraße 4](../../images/eb7e75d34eea56002ef38fe5d381e992_original.jpg)
*1905 — Author: Hermann Bückling | License: HMF | Description: Schloßstraße 4*

The property at Schloßstraße 4 originally belonged to Klara and Sally Oppenheimer. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
