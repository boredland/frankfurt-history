# Stolen Place

*Kaiserstraße 44*

![Kaiserstraße 44](../../images/3a8f873be37bc5e8ce899e951aa98ca1_original.jpg)
*ca. 1907 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kaiserstraße 44*

The property at Kaiserstrasse 44 had originally belonged to Dr. Benno Arnsbacher. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
