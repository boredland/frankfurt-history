# Stolen Place

*Kleine Seestraße 18*

![Kleine Seestraße 18](../../images/981f95ef40591733019ccd9222fdc96b_original.jpg)
*1976 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kleine Seestraße 18*

The property at Kleine Seestrasse 18 originally belonged to Leopold and Dr. Albert Hirsch. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
