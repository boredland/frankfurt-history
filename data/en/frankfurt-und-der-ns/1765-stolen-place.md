# Stolen Place

*Senckenberganlage 36*

![Senckenberganlage 22](../../images/a2f4c877749661a855caa76d177beaf4_original.jpg)
*before 1944 — Author: Südwestdeutsche Luftverkehrs AG | License: ISG | Description: Senckenberganlage 22*

With a purchase agreement dated December 21, 1938, the city acquired the developed property at Senckenberganlage 36 with an area of 716 square meters from the company Adler & Oppenheimer for the price of 45,000 Reichsmark. The company had Jewish owners. The property was intended for university purposes.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
