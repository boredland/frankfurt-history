# Stolen Place

*Leerbachstraße 32*

![Leerbachstraße 32](../../images/f88d6ce9991d8a87e821efccae00e148_original.jpg)
*1890 — Author: Karl Hertel | License: ISG | Description: Leerbachstraße 32*

The property at Leerbachstraße 32 had originally belonged to Otto Scharff. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
