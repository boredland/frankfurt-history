# forced labour camp

*Eintracht-Stadion Riederwald*

![Picture postcard with the Riederwald stadium](../../images/a0ca46616632c69f542fea340b1e5b28_original.jpg)
*after 1937 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, Sammlung Church, C80399 | Description: Picture postcard with the Riederwald stadium*

The Eintracht stadium at Riederwald was the location of a camp for forced labourers who worked for the Opel customer service centre Auto-Schaum at Hanauer Landstrasse 295. In 1942 it lodged 20 Russian men and 1 Russian woman, in 1943 25 Ukrainian men.
