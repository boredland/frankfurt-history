# Stolen Place

*Ostbahnhofstraße 9*

![Ostbahnhofstraße 9](../../images/4a2f077876b9419a968236e7a2c5ff67_original.jpg)
*2016 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Ostbahnhofstraße 9*

The property at Ostbahnhofstraße 9 originally belonged to Moritz Baum. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
