# Stolen Place

*Bethmannstraße 58*

![Bethmannstraße 58](../../images/f7bda4dadfb3703b46b37d39c79ab1c2_original.jpg)
*1935 — Author: Paul Wolff | License: ISG, Frankfurt Best. S7Wo Nr. 3224 | Description: Bethmannstraße 58*

The property at Bethmannstrasse 58 / corner of Kaiserstrasse originally belonged to a Jewish owner. Due to persecution, he had to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
