# Stolen Place

*Landgrafenstraße 28*

![Landgrafenstraße 28](../../images/26d6330237a2646af496ed9d1b00a9d3_original.jpg)
*1906 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Landgrafenstraße 28*

The property at Landgrafenstraße 28 had originally belonged to Dr. Siegfried, known as Fritz Gutenstein. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
