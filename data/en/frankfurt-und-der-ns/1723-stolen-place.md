# Stolen Place

*Mainzer Landstraße 187-189*

![Mainzer Landstraße 187-189](../../images/6c5b14db1a60165c3d5c9943d224584c_original.jpg)
*1933 — Author: Kühn & Olsson | License: ISG | Description: Mainzer Landstraße 187-189*

The property at Mainzer Landstraße 187-189 originally belonged to Elisabeth Schliep, née Blumenthal, who had to transfer it to her granddaughter in 1938 as a result of persecution. Later, the automobile company Dr. Jacobi & Co. ran a camp for civilian workers at Mainzer Landstraße 187.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
