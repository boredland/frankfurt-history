# forced labour camp

*Eschersheimer Landstraße 587*

![Forced labor camp](../../images/21842482353d4802ddac18b4af16a66b_original.jpg)
*1936 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Z Nr. 1936-450 | Description: Forced labor camp*

The Ludwig-Richter-Schule, Eschersheimer Landstraße 587, served as quarters for a construction and labor battalion of Soviet prisoners of war who had probably been transferred here from Stalag IX B Wegscheide or Stalag IX Ziegenhain.
