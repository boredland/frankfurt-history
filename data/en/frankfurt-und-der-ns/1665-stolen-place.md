# Stolen Place

*Grüneburgweg 95*

![Grüneburgweg 95](../../images/065929144b1e72b04676ca0157861872_original.jpg)
*1970 — Author: Günter Moos | License: ISG | Description: Grüneburgweg 95*

The property at Grüneburgweg 95 had originally belonged to Karl Stettenheimer. He was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
