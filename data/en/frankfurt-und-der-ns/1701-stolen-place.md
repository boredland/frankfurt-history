# Stolen Place

*Kleebergstraße 6*

![Kleebergstraße 6](../../images/dec2be0eb16cee7793a7ff785d588ded_original.jpg)
*ca. 1914 — Author: Musch & May | License: ISG | Description: Kleebergstraße 6*

The property at Kleebergstraße 6 had originally belonged to Flora Bickart, née Haymann. She was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
