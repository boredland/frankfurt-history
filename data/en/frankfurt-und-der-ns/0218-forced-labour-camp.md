# forced labour camp

*Sandgasse 6*

![Forced labor camp, Sandgasse 6](../../images/13f5065ebe88e147bd3bd3776cc44a55_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-3896 | Description: Forced labor camp, Sandgasse 6*

The Fritz Kamp uniform manufacturer of Frankfurt was based at this site (now Sandgasse 6). The company exploited at least 4 forced labourers who had their lodgings in two jointly used camps at Allerheiligenstrasse 26 and Reuterweg 91.
