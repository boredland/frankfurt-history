# Stolen Place

*Große Gallusstraße 1*

![Große Gallusstraße 1](../../images/b39a9453f1f7aeea41f01a3c72a66ec7_original.jpg)
*1895 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Große Gallusstraße 1*

The property at Großer Gallusstraße 1 had originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
