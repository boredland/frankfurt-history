# Forced labour camp

*Sondershausenstraße*

![Placeholder image](../../images/Hier%20fehlt_dfdc6d631f271e842082099187aa5623_original.jpg)
*License: HMF*

Sondershausenstrasse was the site of lodgings for labour detachments made up of prisoners of war and forced labourers. Barracks had presumably been erected on the sports field of the post office sports club to house them. They were exploited by the Reich Railway, the Alfred Teves machine and fixtures factory, and the telecommunications company Telefonbau & Normalzeit Lehner & Co. In September 1942, 31 Russians were registered here.
