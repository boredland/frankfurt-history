# DAF local administration

*Kurzröderstraße 18*

![Kurzröderstraße 18](../../images/21c01a8577337b3fb60771d7113d541b_original.jpg)
*2024 — Description: Kurzröderstraße 18*

On the site of today's daycare center used to be the clubhouse that housed the local administration of the German Labor Front (DAF) in Eckenheim. With 25 million members, the German Labour Front was the largest organization in National Socialist Germany. All workers were supposed to organize themselves here. Trade unions had been banned since 1933. The aim of the DAF was to form a unified “national community”.
