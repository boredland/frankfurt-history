# Forced labour camp

*Pfortenstraße 55*

![Picture postcard with the Fechenheim gymnasium](../../images/353e2f0d70327852a883559075a0ae2e_original.jpg)
*before 1955 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S17 Nr. 00796-14 | Description: Picture postcard with the Fechenheim gymnasium*

Meuser & Co., which was based at Hanauer Landstrasse 475, operated a camp for French forced labourers in the gymnasium of the Fechenheim 1885 sports club from February 1943 onwards. Initially the camp lodged 35 persons; in the course of the year 1943 the number grew to 53.
