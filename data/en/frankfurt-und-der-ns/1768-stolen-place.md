# Stolen Place

*Sternstraße 8*

![Sternstraße 8](../../images/d8052baf7aff43db0ca7c519300aee42_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: Leonhard Kleemann | License: HMF | Description: Sternstraße 8*

The property at Sternstraße 8 originally belonged to Heinz Königsberger and Ellen Schwarz, née Königsberger. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
