# Memorial plaque synagogue Friedberger Anlage

*New Jewish cemetery*

![](../../images/243438abf4ef4d16c4d7b40936b570f8_original.jpg)
*2023*

Inside the Jewish cemetery, there is a memorial plaque on the right-hand side of the entrance area. It commemorates the synagogue in the Friedberger Anlage. This synagogue was destroyed during the November pogroms of 1938 by the National Socialists with the participation of Frankfurt citizens. The plaque was erected by the US administration in 1946 at the former site of the synagogue. It is one of three such plaques in the Frankfurt city area. It was moved to the cemetery after 1988, when a larger installation was erected at the Friedberg site to commemorate the synagogue.
