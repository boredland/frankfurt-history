# forced labour camp

*Luxemburger Allee 1-3*

![Forced labor camp, Luxemburger Allee 1-3](../../images/2a8a8c682a7ba33d5e5f2bcabc97395e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1939 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-19131 | Description: Forced labor camp, Luxemburger Allee 1-3*

The first and second companies of the construction and labour battalion 42 were prisoner-of-war labour detachments assigned to clean up after bombing attacks. The prisoners were lodged in the Brüder Grimm School at Luxemburger Allee 1–3.
