# Stolen Place

*Kelsterbacher Straße 6*

![Kelsterbacher Straße 6](../../images/1a27a846a4db5e1a2b69c8716d76725d_original.jpg)
*1907 — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Kelsterbacher Straße 6*

The property at Kelsterbacher Straße 6 originally belonged to Jewish owners. It had to be sold due to persecution. Later, the properties at Kelsterbacher Straße 2-6 were used as a camp for "Eastern workers" who were deployed at the Fritz Emmerich chain factory in central Germany.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
