# Hachshara Center

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 244*

![](../../images/59c1bdaebf755bea30710070bd281672_original.jpg)
*2023*

After 1933, there was a "Hachshara" on this property, a nursery and training center for Jews preparing for emigration to Palestine. In 1942, the establishment was closed by the National Socialist city administration. The nursery was transferred to the municipal gardening department.
