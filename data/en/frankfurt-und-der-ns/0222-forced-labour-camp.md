# forced labour camp

*Hainer Weg 129-131*

![Forced labor camp, Hainer Weg 129-131](../../images/f4cd9f29c5ee2c5737fc4510ec2d671c_original.jpg)
*1935 — Author: Paul Wolff | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Wo Nr. 2780 | Description: Forced labor camp, Hainer Weg 129-131*

The Voltohm rope and cable works had its company grounds at this site. In the summer of 1942, it built barracks here for the purpose of setting up a forced labour camp. According to the Institut für Stadtgeschichte (Institute of Municipal History), 54 forced labourers from Ukraine, 18 of whom were women, were registered here in the summer of 1943.
