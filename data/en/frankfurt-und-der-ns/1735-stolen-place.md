# Stolen Place

*Musikantenweg 61*

![Musikantenweg 61](../../images/a81804f2df7c7237abe163adebd41bfd_original.jpg)
*2012 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Musikantenweg 61*

The property at Musikantenweg 61 had originally belonged to Fanny Sommer, née Karpf. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
