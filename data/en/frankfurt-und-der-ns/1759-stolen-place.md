# Stolen Place

*Schumannstraße 24-26*

![Schumannstraße 24-26](../../images/657e3558ac6252c92407c9242498e4b7_original.jpg)
*ca. 1904 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Schumannstraße 24-26*

The property at Schumannstraße 24-26 originally belonged to Steward Thornhill. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
