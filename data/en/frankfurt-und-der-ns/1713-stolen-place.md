# Stolen Place

*Leerbachstraße 112*

![Leerbachstraße 112](../../images/f9e79e4f656e66a67a144f0ed8750e4d_original.jpg)
*1905 — Author: August Ravenstein | License: ISG | Description: Leerbachstraße 112*

The property at Leerbachstraße 112 had originally belonged to Dr. Berthold and Thekla Bachrach. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
