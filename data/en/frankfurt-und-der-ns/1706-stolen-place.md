# Stolen Place

*Kronberger Straße 33*

![Kronberger Straße 33](../../images/50bb661beca68aae5c487998466b6f58_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kronberger Straße 33*

The property at Kronbergerstraße 33 originally belonged to Otto Brumlik. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939. On December 18, 1939, the expert committee for property issues rejected the sale of the property.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
