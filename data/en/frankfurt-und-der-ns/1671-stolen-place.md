# Stolen Place

*Gutleutstraße 40*

![Gutleutstraße 40](../../images/5cbd88378161d8ae4b99f9aa7442a873_original.jpg)
*1922 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Gutleutstraße 40*

In a purchase agreement dated November 22, 1938, the city acquired the property at Gutleutstrasse 40 from the bankrupt Adler company, which belonged to Jewish owners, for the price of 362,000 Reichsmarks. The property was intended for a craftsmen's house.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
