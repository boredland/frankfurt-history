# Stolen Place

*Hansaallee 30*

![Hansaallee 30](../../images/2c83c2ca283563b9e0a21733a7cf5a58_original.jpg)
*ca. 1970 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Hansaallee 30*

The property at Hansaallee 30 had originally belonged to Julius Wolf and his wife Emilie, née Weyl. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
