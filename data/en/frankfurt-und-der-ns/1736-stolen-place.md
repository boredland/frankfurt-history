# Stolen Place

*Neckarstraße 15*

![Neckarstraße 15](../../images/4cef3226294aed64fe8574dcbab1a31e_original.jpg)
*1916 — Author: Briefkopf | License: ISG | Description: Neckarstraße 15*

The property at Neckarstraße 15 had originally belonged to Rosalie Ganz, née Hamburg. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to transfer her house to two relatives as a gift in 1939.
