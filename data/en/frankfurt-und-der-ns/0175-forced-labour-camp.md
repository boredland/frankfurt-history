# forced labour camp

*Auf der Insel 16*

![Forced labor camp](../../images/f93698d8f4962fab47c831713496cb5a_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7B Nr. 1998-5206 | Description: Forced labor camp*

Lanninger-Regner AG, which manufactured sprinkler systems and fittings for fire extinguishing purposes, ran a camp for forced laborers on Insel 16 (Villa Brentano).
