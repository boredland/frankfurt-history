# Forced labour camp

*Stiftstraße 35-37*

![frankfurthistory@stadt-frankfurt.de](../../images/1e0b7c79a351939d86554bd72a5c51a4_original.jpg)
*License: HMF | Description: frankfurthistory@stadt-frankfurt.de*

More than 60 persons were forced to perform labour for the Ernst F. Ambrosius & Sohn construction company at this site. The labourers had their lodgings in a camp at Alt-Nied 2 (corner Oeserstrasse, Nied train repair workshop). In 1942, 20 Belgians and 16 Dutchmen are known to have lived there; in 1943 16 Russians, 6 Dutchmen, 3 Italians, and 1 Frenchman were registered as living in this camp.
