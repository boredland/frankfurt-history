# Factory site of a forced labor plant

*Gräfstraße 103*

![Factory premises of a forced labour camp, Gräfstraße 103](../../images/eca7e3564dbaec3a8df8cf356a36f584_original.jpg)
*1920 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-19962 | Description: Factory premises of a forced labour camp, Gräfstraße 103*

In 1943, the VDO Tachometer company made use of 110 Soviet prisoners of war in its factory at Königsstrasse 103 (now Gräfstrasse) and lodged them in the Forsthaus Biegwald. VDO also exploited forced labourers from Poland.
