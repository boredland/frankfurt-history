# Stern siblings

*5 Places – 5 Stories*

![Sandweg 28 (today)](../../images/edcf3929492ba27f3ee229ec0a45ab08_original.jpg)
*2023 — Author: Lisa Lungu, Laura Wand | Description: Sandweg 28 (today)*

The house of the Stern family could be found in Sandweg 28. The four sisters Irma (May 10, 1886), Ella (May 11, 1886), Flora (January 29, 1884), and Rosa Stern (December 3, 1880) lived there. Due to debt, they were forced to sell their house. The siblings died in Riga. It can be assumed that they were deported to Riga, as thousands of innocent Jewish people were taken there by the National Socialists and died there. In overcrowded housing estates, the Jewish people fought against hunger, disease and mistreatments. Many were deported to the woods and shot during the Rumbula massacre in November/December 1941.
