# Stolen Place

*Zeppelinallee 41*

![Zeppelinallee 41](../../images/99140f447b5753f1cd841fea8a4bd084_original.jpg)
*ca. 1911 — Author: August Ravenstein | License: ISG | Description: Zeppelinallee 41*

The property at Zeppelinallee 41 originally belonged to Richard Weil and his wife Paula, née Höchstädter. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
