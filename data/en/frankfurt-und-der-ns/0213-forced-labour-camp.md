# forced labour camp

*Lenaustraße 64-68*

![Picture postcard with exterior view of the Glauburg School](../../images/16ba277a9f1c84cecb224e9edce095db_original.jpg)
*1910 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, Sammlung Church, C80407 | Description: Picture postcard with exterior view of the Glauburg School*

In 1944 and 1945, there was a forced labour camp in the Glauburg School on Glauburgsplatz (now Lenaustrasse 64–70). The “Glazer Battalion XII”, which was made up of prisoners of war, was lodged there. The POWs were put to work cleaning up the damages caused by bombing in the city.
