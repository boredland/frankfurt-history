# Bomb shelter

*Ludwigstraße*

![Photo of a bomb shelter located in Ludwigstraße](../../images/e65009731c342e8dcfdf021c82dceb61_original.jpg)
*1944 — Author: unbekannt | License: Denkmalamt Stadt Frankfurt | Description: Photo of a bomb shelter located in Ludwigstraße*

This was the site of the city’s police headquarters until 2002. At the rear entrance there was a bomb shelter for the leadership elite of the local branch of the Nazi party as well as the city’s air-raid warning service and air-raid wardens. The chief of police doubled as the chief municipal air-raid warden. In this capacity he coordinated fire-extinguishing and rescue/recovery operations. The shelter provided space for 300 persons on four floors. Construction began in March 1944. It took 39 labourers 19 months to build the structure, which was torn down in 1971.

 Construction company: Karl Hofmann II, Ortenberg
 Architect: Fritz Maier, Frankfurt
