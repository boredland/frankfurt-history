# Kompostellhof

*Dominikanergasse*

![Photo of the Kompostellhof](../../images/9d29abe26c9ed3500d7c063a6297cbf2_original.jpg)
*1933 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-27828 | Description: Photo of the Kompostellhof*

The “Kompostellhof” was a spacious location for events and functions. It had a large hall to offer, with capacity for 600 persons, as well as many smaller rooms and club pubs. The members of the National Socialist German Workers’ Party gathered here between 1922 and 1925. It was one of the first locations used by the Nazi party in Frankfurt for its meetings. There were numerous Jewish institutions and synagogues in the immediate vicinity.
