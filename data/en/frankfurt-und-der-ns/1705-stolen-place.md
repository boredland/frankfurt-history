# Stolen Place

*Kornmarkt 4*

![Kornmarkt 4](../../images/fa4ccea5e9072431114ef18e88dfd94f_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kornmarkt 4*

With a purchase agreement dated June 11, 1938, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 841 square meters at Großer Kornmarkt 18 (today approximately Konrmarkt 4) from Gebrüder Seckel o.H.G. in liquidation, which had Jewish owners, for the price of 92,000 Reichsmark. The property was intended for official offices.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
