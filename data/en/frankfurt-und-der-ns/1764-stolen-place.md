# Stolen Place

*Senckenberganlage 22*

![Senckenberganlage 22](../../images/fe6b0ff58ee860833b9666a27c0a5ee1_original.jpg)
*before 1944 — Author: Südwestdeutsche Luftverkehrs AG | License: ISG | Description: Senckenberganlage 22*

With a purchase agreement dated September 28, 1938, the city acquired the developed property at Senckenberganlage 22 with an area of 927 square meters from Dr. M. Seligmann and his wife, who had been persecuted for anti-Semitic reasons, for the price of 65,000 Reichsmarks. The property was to be used for university purposes.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
