# forced labour camp

*Rebstöcker Weg 17*

![Forced labor camp](../../images/7b5935511706956c970f8c81fca8abdc_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2006-340 | Description: Forced labor camp*

The grounds at Am Biegwald (now Rebstöcker Strasse 17) belonged to the “Vorwärts” gymnastics club. The municipal building department for air-raid protection set up a camp here for forced labourers assigned to carry out clean-up work after bombing attacks. For the autumn of 1942, 66 Dutch men and 9 Lithuanian men are documented as inhabitants, for 1943 96 forced labourers from the Netherlands, France, Belgium, the so-called Protectorate of Bohemia and Moravia (now Czech Republic), and Croatia.
