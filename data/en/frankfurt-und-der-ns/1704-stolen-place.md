# Stolen Place

*Königswarterstraße 25*

![Königswarterstraße 25](../../images/bdc6ff98affce77a0e8177c1bda92872_original.jpg)
*2012 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Königswarterstraße 25*

The property at Quinkestraße 25 (now Königswarther Straße) originally belonged to Rahel Mainz. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
