# forced labour camp

*Falkstraße 34*

![Forced labor camp, Falkstraße 34](../../images/9566e0c68970623079c41b73d9ee49c9_original.jpg)
*ca. 1905 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 2001-178 | Description: Forced labor camp, Falkstraße 34*

Hartmann & Braun operated a camp for forced labourers here. Based at Königstrasse 97 (now Gräfstrasse), the company was a manufacturer and supplier for the aircraft industry. Between April 1942 and September 1944, 120 forced labourers are thought to have been lodged here.
