# forced labour camp

*Mainzer Landstraße 27*

![Forced labor camp, Mainzer Landstraße 27](../../images/9f4bce829042560b794655b95f360dcf_original.jpg)
*1933 — Author: Hannah Reeck | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-11192 | Description: Forced labor camp, Mainzer Landstraße 27*

W. Schmidt-Diehler & Co., which engaged in building construction, was based at Am Blittersdorfplatz 29 (now Mainzer Landstrasse). At Fischerfeldstrasse 13 and Rückertstrasse 53 it operated two joint camps about which no further information is available.
