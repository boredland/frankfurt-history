# Stolen Place

*Hermesweg 3*

![Hermesweg 3](../../images/c2daee7e12826e566cea682ac5ce0bda_original.jpg)
*2012 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Hermesweg 3*

The property at Hermesweg 3 originally belonged to Rosa Hammelburger. She was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1941.

 

Research: Jutta Zwilling
