# Stolen Place

*Loenstraße 3*

![Loenstraße 3](../../images/cd986cd5a7f0e102567473cf7cdde11d_original.jpg)
*1983 — Author: Jutta Hofmann | License: ISG | Description: Loenstraße 3*

Half of the property at Loenstraße 3 had originally belonged to Georg Heymann. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to transfer his co-ownership share of the house to non-Jewish relatives as a gift in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
