# forced labour camp

*Hahnstraße 30-34*

![Forced labor camp, Hahnstraße 30-34](../../images/e8f5efa120b841eec8be90d0dd9b6f0e_original.jpg)
*1925 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-29791 | Description: Forced labor camp, Hahnstraße 30-34*

The Johannes Holzhauer canning factory was located at Kesselbergstrasse 30–34 (now Hahnstrasse). Starting in 1942, the company exploited some 20 female forced labourers from Russia and Ukraine. It is not known where these women had their accommodations.
