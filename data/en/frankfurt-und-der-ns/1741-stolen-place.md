# Stolen Place

*Oeder Weg 113*

![Oeder Weg 113](../../images/b198b1561a59b5794d44fb38f1aa4abf_original.jpg)
*1924 — Author: Carl Oehler | License: ISG | Description: Oeder Weg 113*

The property at Oeder Weg 113 had originally belonged to Adolf Hamburger and his wife Rosa, née Marx. They had to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938 due to anti-Semitic persecution.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
