# NSV local group Eckenheim

*Engeltaler Straße 34*

![](../../images/6348042a4965b5011b567c0ac3ce80c1_original.jpg)

This building was the headquarters of the local branch of the National Socialist People's Welfare Organization (NSV). This was a social network for "people's comrades". For example, it organized "Kinderlandverschickungen" and "Mütterdienst". Only people who were considered "Aryan" Germans according to Nazi racial doctrine were supported, i.e. no Jews, Sinti and Roma or foreigners.
