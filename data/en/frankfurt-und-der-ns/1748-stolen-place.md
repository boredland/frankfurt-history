# Stolen Place

*Reinganumstraße 27*

![Reinganumstraße 27](../../images/7d0cee94a70c6b9cd82f75aca009b69b_original.jpg)
*2017 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Reinganumstraße 27*

The property at Raumerstraße 27 (now Reinganumstraße) originally belonged to the heirs of Leo Wreschner. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
