# Frankfurt main post office

*Zeil 106-110*

![Photo of the main post office](../../images/60e79eeee0ff0278e4629ac517f8ce24_original.jpg)
*1942 — Author: Fred Kochmann | License: HMF Ph00665 | Description: Photo of the main post office*

The Main Post Office had a secret post office box allowing the Gestapo to maintain contact with its agents and spies. The information exchanged via this PO box led to many people critical of the Nazi regime being discovered, arrested and, in some cases, deported.
