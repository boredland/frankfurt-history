# forced labour camp

*Hausener Obergasse 49*

![Forced labor camp, Hausener Obergasse 49](../../images/1b2486ae29e2353c0d0bd5a8a5837027_original.jpg)
*1934 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-21742 | Description: Forced labor camp, Hausener Obergasse 49*

Under National Socialism, the Georg Vicari & Co. furniture factory based at Alt-Hausen 34, corner of Am Ellerfeld profited from forced labour. The company had two forced labour camps: one here at Obergasse 49, about which no information has been found, and another at Hausener Obergasse 7. In 1942, 19 forced labourers from Italy were lodged there, in 1943 9 forced labourers from France, 1 from Belgium, 3 from the Netherlands, and 7 from Italy.
