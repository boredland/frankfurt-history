# Stolen Place

*Röderbergweg 62-64*

![Röderbergweg 62-64](../../images/c2096363ccdd92b74220dadc33d4ad46_original.jpg)
*ca. 1925 — License: ISG | Description: Röderbergweg 62-64*

With a contract dated June 10, 1942, the city acquired the Gumpertzsche Siechenhaus with an area of 2,816 square meters from the Reich Association of Jews in Germany, in which the Jewish Community and the Israelite Religious Community had been forcibly united, for the price of 155,440 Reichsmark. The property was to be used as a teaching kitchen.
