# Stolen Place

*Niedenau 63*

![Niedenau 63](../../images/29a94ea42ac5fd7313672b813c4b9365_original.jpg)
*1971 — Author: TH Darmstadt | License: HMF | Description: Niedenau 63*

The property at Niedenau 63 had originally belonged to Saly Sundheimer and his wife Hermine, née Uhlfelder. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
