# Stolen Place

*Oskar-von-Miller-Straße 5*

![Oskar-von-Miller-Straße 5](../../images/d88fcc54139d82a557ed64e654855131_original.jpg)
*1983 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Oskar-von-Miller-Straße 5*

The property at Oskar-von-Miller-Straße 5 had originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
