# Forced labour camp

*Westerbachstraße 47*

![Photo of a workshop on the premises of Deutsche Vereinigte Schuhmaschinenfabrik GmbH (DVSG), where a forced labour camp was operated](../../images/ee42c6000a42c63949daca7637066b87_original.jpg)
*ca. 1938 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-29295 | Description: Photo of a workshop on the premises of Deutsche Vereinigte Schuhmaschinenfabrik GmbH (DVSG), where a forced labour camp was operated*

The Deutsche Vereinigte Schuhmaschinenfabrik, a shoe machine factory, operated a forced labour camp on its factory grounds at Westerbachstrasse 47. The forced labourers assigned to work for the company had come from two other camps: in 1943, 50 forced labourers from France and Belgium had been lodged in the camp of the Chamber of Industry and Trade (address unknown). According to a police report, on 15 May 1945 there were still some 300 forced labourers from Russia in the jointly used camp at Eschborner Landstrasse 20–22, awaiting their homeward journey.
