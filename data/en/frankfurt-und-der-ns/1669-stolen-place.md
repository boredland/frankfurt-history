# Stolen Place

*Guiollettstraße 24*

![Guiollettstraße 24](../../images/d76636743aba48a9571a715e13837e9d_original.jpg)
*ca. 1968 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Guiollettstraße 24*

The property at Guiollettstraße 24 had originally belonged to Dr. Alfred Mertion and his wife Clotilde, née Schmidt. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1941.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
