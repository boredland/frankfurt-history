# forced labour camp

*Domplatz 8*

![Forced labor camp, Domplatz 8](../../images/C27974_jpg_0ccb0178e641afeceb55082684692659_original.jpg)
*1932 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, C27974 | Description: Forced labor camp, Domplatz 8*

The Brehler bed company operated a camp for civilian workers at Domplatz 8. Civilian workers were forced labourers who were neither prisoners of war nor concentration camp inmates. They were likewise often unfree and deprived of their rights. In 1944, 2 Ukrainian women had their lodgings here.
