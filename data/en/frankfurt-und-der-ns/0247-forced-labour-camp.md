# forced labour camp

*Lorscher Straße 8*

![Picture postcard showing Lorscher Straße in Frankfurt-Rödelheim](../../images/7d86a4eb990242af5fb4773f60e66c7d_original.jpg)
*1927 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, Sammlung Church, C80400 | Description: Picture postcard showing Lorscher Straße in Frankfurt-Rödelheim*

The Hartmann & Braun company, which manufactured electrical and thermo-technical measuring instruments, profited from forced labour. During World War II it operated ten camps in Frankfurt. One of them was here at Lorscher Strasse 8, corner of Alexanderstrasse in the Hotel Deutscher Hof. Some 200 forced labourers from France were lodged here between 1941 and 1944. In 1942, 58 men lived here, in 1943 78 women, including 1 from Belgium and 1 from Poland.
