# Arrest of W. Heinrich on Kai­ser­straße

*Kaiserstraße*

![Postcard of Kaiserstraße with a view towards the main railway station](../../images/75dbf43bc3ce17bb47efad74b8d93196_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: Verlag Emil Hartmann | License: Historisches Museum Frankfurt | Description: Postcard of Kaiserstraße with a view towards the main railway station*

Located in Frankfurt’s inner city, the Kaiserstraße was a prestigious boulevard dating from the early days of Imperial Germany.

In January 1925, after causing a gathering of a crowd of around 50 people, the 16-year-old trans* person W. Heinrich was arrested in women’s clothing on Kaiserstraße. The police file includes the entry: “Passers-by shouted it was scandalous that the police did not clamp down on such streetwalkers.”

*Author: Clara Hartmann*
