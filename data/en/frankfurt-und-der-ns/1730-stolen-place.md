# Stolen Place

*Melemstraße 7*

![Melemstraße 7](../../images/dc8bf0d71f3f86bb305295e2aa60878e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1925 — Author: Hofmann & Müller | License: ISG | Description: Melemstraße 7*

The property at Melemstraße 7 originally belonged to Rosa Hirschmann, née Ambach, who had to sell it in 1939 due to persecution. Later, the company Bauausführungen Peter Schäfer ran a camp for civilian workers here.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
