# Stolen Place

*Westendstraße 72*

![Westendstraße 72](../../images/23445a856060068cf7f0500c7f926c7d_original.jpg)
*1896 — Author: Ludwig Kopf | License: ISG | Description: Westendstraße 72*

The property at Westendstraße 72 originally belonged to Maria Durlacher, née Wormser. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
