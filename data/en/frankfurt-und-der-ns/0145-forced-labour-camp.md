# forced labour camp

*Alt Sossenheim 27*

![Forced labor camp, Alt Sossenheim 27](../../images/e72da012c81b5e3935bfff3e6fbfbcf9_original.jpg)
*ca. 1950 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 2012-16 | Description: Forced labor camp, Alt Sossenheim 27*

This was the location of the Zur Rose guesthouse. The Ada-Ada shoe company operated one of its two forced labour camps at this site. In 1942, 17 men from Lithuania and 1 from France had their lodgings here.
