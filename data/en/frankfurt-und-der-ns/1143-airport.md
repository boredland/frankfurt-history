# Airport

*Inclusive educational vacation "Your city and National Socialism"*

Inklusiver Bildungsurlaub „Deine Stadt und der Nationalsozialismus“

![](../../images/isg_ffm_s7_72c1fa3537580aaff15a9a6a1480e3a9_original.jpg)

This video was created as part of the inclusive educational vacation "Your City and National Socialism" in summer 2022. Participants with and without disabilities explored the exhibition of the Historical Museum together, talked about their own references to National Socialism and examined the urban space for traces of National Socialism today. The self-written and shot films document the results of the respective groups. The project was initiated as part of "Frankfurt and National Socialism. Eine Gedächtnisplattform" (Frankfurt and National Socialism: A Platform for Remembrance), carried out in cooperation by the Frankfurt Historical Museum, the Volkshochschule Frankfurt am Main and Lebenshilfe Frankfurt, and funded by the Foundation Erinnerung, Verantwortung, Zukunft (EVZ) and the Bundesfinanzministerium (BMF).
