# Stolen Place

*Mathildenstraße 12*

![Mathildenstraße 12](../../images/1f78841f2d0643edf33bd85e16837332_original.jpg)
*2002 — Author: Claus Graubner | License: ISG | Description: Mathildenstraße 12*

The property at Mathildenstraße 12 had originally belonged to Hugo Hochschild. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
