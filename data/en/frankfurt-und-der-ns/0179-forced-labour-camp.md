# forced labour camp

*Bethmannstraße 33*

![Forced labor camp, Bethmannstraße 33](../../images/d4560504f1d9cb42869ed12de693ae86_original.jpg)
*unbekannt — Author: Wolff & Tritschler | License: HMF, C35249 | Description: Forced labor camp, Bethmannstraße 33*

Forced labourers from all over Europe were put to work at the Hotel Frankfurter Hof, which was run by the Steigenberger company. It is unclear where they had their lodgings. We do know, however, that in September 1942 at least 21 forced labourers from France, Belgium, Holland, Italy, Spain, Croatia, Armenia, and Switzerland were working here. In 1943 the number increased to 164, among them 70 German men and 54 German women.
