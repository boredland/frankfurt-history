# Stolen Place

*Leerbachstraße 13*

![Leerbachstraße 13](../../images/b88f7f187cb61495c79f367397eeb87e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1935 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Leerbachstraße 13*

The property at Leerbachstraße 13 had originally belonged to Claire Halphen, née Istel, and André Istel. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939. They sold the building on to the metal company in 1943.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
