# Residence of Johanna Klawuhn

*Kleine Rittergasse 39*

![](../../images/519eb9cefd8bee12c79f440cf500c144_original.jpg)

This was the home of Johanna Klawuhn, née Wiegand (1896–unknown). Her story is an example of the persecution of mentally ill persons under National Socialism.
