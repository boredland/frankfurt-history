# New Jewish cemetery

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 238*

Graves of suicide victims on the main path

![](../../images/c8794161e1d708bf50bf64bfcabdb24d_original.jpg)
*2023*

Here, 800 gravestones along the main path commemorate people who took their own lives during the Nazi era. Persecution, humiliation, the threat of deportation or the fear of dying in agony in a concentration camp were probably the reasons for these acts of desperation. Immediately after the November pogrom of 1938, the number of "suicide victims" rose sharply. German "Volksgenossen" destroyed Jewish institutions, synagogues, but also homes and business premises. The police deported Jewish men to concentration camps. Life was made impossible for Jewish Germans.
