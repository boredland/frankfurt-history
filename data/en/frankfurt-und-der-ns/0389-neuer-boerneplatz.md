# Neuer Börneplatz

*Video school theater studio*

Video Schultheater-Studio

![](../../images/00021fae60d17cd1c3652e80659f189d_original.jpg)

The video was created as part of the project "What does May 8 mean to me?". Five senior drama and history classes from the Carl Schurz and Tony Sender schools were out and about in Frankfurt to document places of persecution, resistance, Jewish life, places of perpetrators and lost places from the National Socialist era. The project was initiated as part of "Frankfurt and National Socialism. A Memory Platform" and funded by the Foundation Remembrance, Responsibility and Future (EVZ) and the Federal Ministry of Finance (BMF).
