# Stolen Place

*Guiollettstraße 34*

![Guiollettstraße 34](../../images/0b0f647154e1ccebd9a70bc5ec95cb28_original.jpg)
*ca. 1970 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Guiollettstraße 34*

The property at Guiollettstraße 34 had originally belonged to Albert Ullmann. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.
