# Forced labour camp

*Wiener Straße 26*

![Photograph of a forced labour camp operated by Fischer AG](../../images/2d40130fea50ab84ee69f0ae6198f475_original.jpg)
*ca. 1935 — Author: AERO-Express | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-24003 | Description: Photograph of a forced labour camp operated by Fischer AG*

Wiener Strasse 24 or 26 was the site of a forced labour camp of the Fischer company, an apparatus manufacturer that had its factory grounds at Offenbacher Landstrasse 368. In 1943 the company made use of 20 forced labourers from Ukraine and lodged them here.
