# Stolen Place

*Städelstraße 6*

![Städelstraße 6](../../images/1e67a48644ee64fdfdfffa1ce5de48fa_original.jpg)
*1912 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Städelstraße 6*

The property at Städelstraße 6 originally belonged to Else Johanna Leiffmann, née Bry. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
