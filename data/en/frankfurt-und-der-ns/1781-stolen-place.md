# Stolen Place

*Westendstraße 85*

![Westendstraße 85](../../images/9630cecd2c8377f6f8f9ae3bb1300ae4_original.jpg)
*before 1950 — Author: Postkartensammlung | License: ISG | Description: Westendstraße 85*

The property at Westendstraße 85 originally belonged to Gertrud, called Grete, Meyer, née Reichenberg. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
