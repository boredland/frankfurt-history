# Sachsenhausen Hospital

*Schulstraße 31*

![Sachsenhausen Private Hospital. ISG FFM, S7A-1998-26853.](../../images/7da6823c1c4fcd8e9293cf3a81c3d27f_original.jpg)
*1912 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte Frankfurt | Description: Sachsenhausen Private Hospital. ISG FFM, S7A-1998-26853.*

In 1928, Max Flesch-Thebesius was appointed head surgeon at the Sachsenhausen private hospital (today part of Frankfurt University Hospital). In October 1931, he operated on the trans woman Hertha Wind. This was not only the first gender affirming surgery here, but also one of the earliest operations of its kind known in Germany, and one of the few outside Berlin.

*Author: Clara Hartmann*
