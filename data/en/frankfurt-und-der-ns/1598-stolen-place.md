# Stolen Place

*Allerheiligenstraße 91*

![Allerheiligenstraße 91](../../images/f0f6821d6e52a540e5ecf450015c332c_original.jpg)
*ca. 1915 — Author: Müller-May | License: ISG | Description: Allerheiligenstraße 91*

The property at Allerheiligenstraße 91 originally belonged to a Jewish owner. Due to persecution, he had to sell this property as well as Fahrgasse 150 and Allerheiligenstraße 93 to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
