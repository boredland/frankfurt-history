# Stolen Place

*Hanauer Landstraße 12*

![Hanauer Landstraße 12](../../images/4f41174614b2cfc67965a485feeed50a_original.jpg)
*1930 — Author: P. Körbitz | License: ISG | Description: Hanauer Landstraße 12*

The property at Hanauer Landstraße 12 had originally belonged to Max Stern. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
