# Bomb shelter

*Paul-Ehrlich-Straße 40*

![](../../images/tiefbunker_3802888f38af9a4eae8f5c4ad23a26a5_original.jpg)
*Author: Dieter Wesp*

This bomb shelter of the municipal clinic (now the university clinic) in Ludwig-Rehn-Strasse (now Paul-Ehrlich-Strasse) was part of the “Fuehrer’s Emergency Programme”. It housed underground operating rooms and two hospital wards. Construction work lasted from April 1941 to September 1942. The shelter went into operation in 1943 and offered protection to 565 persons on two floors. After the war, the Surgical Polyclinic used the facility until 1972. The original fittings are still partially intact. During the Cold War, the building was kept in good condition. It has been a listed monument since 2012.

Construction company: Diplom-Ingenieur Karl Kögel, Frankfurt
 Architect: August Wilhelm Müller, Frankfurt
