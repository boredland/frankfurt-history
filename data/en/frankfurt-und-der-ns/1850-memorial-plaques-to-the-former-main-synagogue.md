# Memorial plaques to the former main synagogue

*Kurt-Schumacher-Straße 41*

![Der Architekturentwurf der ehemaligen Hauptsynagoge Börnestraße von Johann Georg Kayser.](../../images/abb7fe6b26e395beb43f849feda91141_original.jpg)
*Author: Künstler Johann Georg Kayser | License: © Institut für Stadtgeschichte (öffentlicher Aushang An der Staufermauer), Frankfurt am Main, s7a_nr_1998-18936 | Description: Der Architekturentwurf der ehemaligen Hauptsynagoge Börnestraße von Johann Georg Kayser.*

The former main synagogue was located near Judengasse and served as a religious and cultural centre. It was seen to be symbolic of the significance of reform Judaism in Frankfurt. Destroyed by the National Socialists during the pogrom night in 1938, the former main synagogue is commemorated today by the Neuer Börneplatz memorial site and various commemorative plaques located between Kurt-Schumacher-Strasse and An der Staufenmauer.
