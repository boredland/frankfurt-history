# Stolen Place

*Leerbachstraße 107*

![Leerbachstraße 107](../../images/4f8ba213c44a6e8fc159c595b01f1441_original.jpg)
*1905 — Author: August Ravenstein | License: ISG | Description: Leerbachstraße 107*

The property at Leerbachstraße 107 had originally belonged to Josef Neuhaus. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1940. As the contract was probably not approved by the building authorities, he sold it again in 1941 to another non-Jewish buyer.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
