# Stolen Place

*Liebigstraße 3*

![Liebigstraße 3](../../images/c4e2a688aede1d79644770431ab7b415_original.jpg)
*ca. 1890 — Author: Hertel | License: ISG | Description: Liebigstraße 3*

The property at Liebigstraße 3 had originally belonged to Alice Maas, née Stiebel, and the heirs of Ferdinand Maas. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house in 1939 to a non-Jewish aristocratic buyer and his wife.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
