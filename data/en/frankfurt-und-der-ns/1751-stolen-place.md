# Stolen Place

*Röderbergweg 29*

![Röderbergweg 29](../../images/3000301a6f4df67d51c43de5ba21e1d0_original.jpg)
*ca. 1925 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Röderbergweg 29*

Founded in 1881, the private Jewish elementary school for boys and girls at Röderbergweg 29 / Bärenstraße 11 had to close after the November 1938 pogrom. Under duress, the property had to be sold to the city in 1938. After liberation, Jewish survivors of the concentration and extermination camps (displaced persons) were housed here, among others.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
