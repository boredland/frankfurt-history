# Hospital "Heilig Geist"

*Lange Straße 4-6*

![Holy Spirit Hospital](../../images/10bd1b12f8c828e51ae4cc8bfe9ad1c4_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: BLDAM, Bildarchiv, Neg.Nr. 42 b 30/6441.1 | License: HMF C31454 | Description: Holy Spirit Hospital*

From 1935, medical personnel in the Heilig-Geist-Hospital in Hans-Handwerk-Strasse (now Lange Straße) began conducting forced sterilisations, making people infertile against their will.
