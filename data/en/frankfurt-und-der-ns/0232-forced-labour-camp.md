# forced labour camp

*In der Au 2*

![Forced labor camp, In der Au 2](../../images/a333a5d7fda1123a5acc4f04426d5aec_original.jpg)
*unknown — Author: Paul Wolff | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Wo Nr. 5578 | Description: Forced labor camp, In der Au 2*

Hans Metz & Co., a precision maker of profile steel and lathe tools, had its company grounds at this site. It set up a forced labour camp here in which 8 forced labourers were registered in September 1942. In 1943 the number rose to 32 forced labourers who had been deported here from France, Romania, Yugoslavia, Holland, and Belgium. The camp was in existence until April 1943.
