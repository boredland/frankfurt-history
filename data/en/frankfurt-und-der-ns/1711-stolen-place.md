# Stolen Place

*Lange Straße 29*

![Lange Straße 29](../../images/956cadc7465c797157fe3151e7176285_original.jpg)
*n. d. — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Lange Straße 29*

The property at Hans-Handwerk-Straße 29 (now Lange Straße) originally belonged to Albert Landauer. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his property to a non-Jewish aristocratic buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
