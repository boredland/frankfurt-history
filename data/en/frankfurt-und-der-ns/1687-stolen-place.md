# Stolen Place

*Im Trutz 13*

![Im Trutz 13](../../images/c39446f3f762e1aab9dca4c05f9ebb7a_original.jpg)
*ca. 1880 — Author: unbekannt | License: HMF | Description: Im Trutz 13*

The property at Im Trutz 13 originally belonged to Leo and Siegfried Otto and Hans Arnold Wreschner. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
