# Stolen Place

*Ludolfusstraße 5*

![Ludolfusstraße 5](../../images/1c6f864276e45f3305d0e8c2b19311f5_original.jpg)
*1912 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Ludolfusstraße 5*

The property at Ludolfusstraße 5 had originally belonged to Minna Regenstein. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.
