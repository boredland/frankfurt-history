# forced labour camp

*Hanauer Landstraße 108*

![Forced labour camp, Hanauer Landstraße 108](../../images/6c63536800b23f58ddeb4f2cf5b4eca8_original.jpg)
*1927 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A No. 1998-13997 | Description: Forced labour camp, Hanauer Landstraße 108*

The forced labour camp of a branch of Daimler Benz was located at the corner of Hanauer Landstrasse 108 and Honsellstrasse. The camp lodged approximately 30 persons. Between 1942 and 1944, the factory exploited forced labourers from Russia. The building plan, however, indicates that there were also rooms for Czechs and Italians.
