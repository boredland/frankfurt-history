# Stolen Place

*Leipziger Straße 41*

![Leipziger Straße 41](../../images/827be59a145c1660951280cce3de6a99_original.jpg)
*1906 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Leipziger Straße 41*

The property at Leipziger Strasse 41 / corner of Am Weingarten originally belonged to Leopold and Dr. Albert Hirsch. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
