# forced labor company

*Goldsteinstraße 114*

![Forced labor company, Goldsteinstraße 114](../../images/87b44e13effa52c25fec934b1c2315a3_original.jpg)
*unkown — Author: Paul Wolff | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Wo Nr. 5230 | Description: Forced labor company, Goldsteinstraße 114*

The Franz Garny safe factory exploited some 130 forced labourers who had their lodgings just a few metres away at Goldsteinstrasse 35. The building was the restaurant of the Niederrad gymnastics society. In September 1942, 94 Russians were registered there, in 1943 134 persons from Russia, Ukraine, and Belarus. During World War II, the Garny company also operated a camp at Kesselbergstrasse 50 (now Hahnstrasse).
