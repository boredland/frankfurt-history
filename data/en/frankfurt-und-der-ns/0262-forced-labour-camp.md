# Forced labour camp

*Oeserstraße 12*

![Picture postcard with Nied station](../../images/7542f78183e90a9579b19b0a660e574d_original.jpg)
*n.d. — Author: unbekannt | License: HMF / Sammlung Church C80388 | Description: Picture postcard with Nied station*

The Deutsche Reichsbahn (German Reich Railway) operated a forced labour camp for 24 persons in the waiting room of the Nied station at Oeserstrasse 12. The camp was dissolved in May 1945 after the arrival of the Allies.
