# Stolen Place

*Kaiserstraße 66*

![Kaiserstraße 66](../../images/99a21799302699f927b00d4eb912fe3f_original.jpg)
*about 1900 — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Kaiserstraße 66*

The property at Kaiserstrasse 66 had originally belonged to Adolf Kaufmann and Wilhelm Passmann. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
