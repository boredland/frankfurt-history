# Stolen Place

*Alte Gasse 26a*

![Alte Gasse 26a](../../images/64185d034415999d32afec7874ca4e27_original.jpg)
*1908 — Author: Hermann Bückling | License: HMF | Description: Alte Gasse 26a*

The property at Alte Gasse 26a originally belonged to master butcher Isidor Schönmann, who had run his butcher's shop there. Due to persecution, the Jewish owner had to sell the four-storey building on a plot of 215 square meters with two stores and seven apartments, including the butcher's shop equipment, to a non-Jewish buyer for 39,000 Reichsmark in a purchase contract dated 2 October 1938. The officially tax-assessed value amounted to 52,200 Reichsmark. No compensatory levy to the German Reich was imposed.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
