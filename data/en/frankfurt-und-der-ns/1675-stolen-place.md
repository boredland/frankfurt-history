# Stolen Place

*Hansaallee 12*

![Hansaallee 12](../../images/4259e5aa678aa094e304fe55c47a0050_original.jpg)
*1902 — Author: Philipp Hufnagel | License: ISG | Description: Hansaallee 12*

The property at Hansaallee 12 / corner of Wolfsgangstraße originally belonged to the Ferdinand Baer heirs. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
