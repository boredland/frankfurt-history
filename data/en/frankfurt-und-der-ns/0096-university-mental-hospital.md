# University Mental Hospital

*Heinrich-Hoffmann-Straße 10*

![University psychiatric hospital, Heinrich-Hoffmann-Straße 10](../../images/c628295604a7ff5c1292793470e364c9_original.jpg)
*ca. 1931 — Author: Otto Emmel | License: HMF, Ph35169 | Description: University psychiatric hospital, Heinrich-Hoffmann-Straße 10*

This was the location of the University Psychiatric Clinic, as it was called. Here and at the University Institute for Hereditary Biology and Racial Hygiene, doctors issued numerous expert reports calling for individuals to undergo forced sterilisation procedures. On the basis of these reports, many patients were also committed to so-called psychiatric clinics where they were tortured and killed.
