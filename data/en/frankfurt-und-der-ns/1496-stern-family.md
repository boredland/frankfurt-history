# Stern family

*Kantstrasse 6*

<p>A former "ghetto house"</p>

![Selma and Elfriede Stern](../../images/117ccbb2b058e8ae580f5d610c0b2812_original.jpg)
*Author: unbekannt | Description: Selma and Elfriede Stern*

Selma and Seligmann Stern lived here in Kantstraße 6 with their daughter Elfriede (born in 1914), who was able to emigrate to Palestine in 1935.

When tenant protection for Jewish citizens was abolished, the house became one of almost 300 "ghetto houses" in which Jewish families were forced to settle. In addition to the Sterns, another 18 men, women and children lived here, all of them later deported and murdered.

Seligmann Stern had fought in the First World War and became infected with malaria, which left him seriously ill and in need of care for years. When his wife was imprisoned and he himself harassed by the Gestapo, he saw no way out and committed suicide on 7 July 1941. He is buried in the New Jewish Cemetery.

*Author: Helga Irsch-Breuer*
