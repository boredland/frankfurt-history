# Stolen Place

*Schützenstraße 14*

![Schützenstraße 14](../../images/7ca9a7e52f0181c3e6ed85a144e971fd_original.jpg)
*1932 — Author: Leonhard Kleemann | License: HMF | Description: Schützenstraße 14*

The property at Schützenstraße 14 originally belonged to Emilie Cohn, née Perlstein. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
