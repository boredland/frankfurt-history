# Stolen Place

*Guiollettstraße 17*

![Guiollettstraße 17](../../images/7f5e8eca1de8cc09d0f3ac2c41e51971_original.jpg)
*1931 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Guiollettstraße 17*

The property at Guiollettstraße 17 had originally belonged to Ludwig Gutheim. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
