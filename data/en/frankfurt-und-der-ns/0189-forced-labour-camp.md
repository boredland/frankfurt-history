# forced labour camp

*Schäfergasse 19*

![Forced labour camp, Schäfergasse 19](../../images/51efcbbdbd9735367ca3c80e28c91276_original.jpg)
*ca. 1880 — Author: Hertel | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A 1998-9505 | Description: Forced labour camp, Schäfergasse 19*

This was the location of the Johann Mankel winery. In 1943 the company made use of two forced labourers from Western Europe in its wine shop. Their exact country/countries of origin are unknown. They were lodged in the jointly used camp at Reuterweg 91.
