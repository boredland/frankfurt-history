# forced labor company

*Solmsstraße 81-83*

![Forced labor company, Solmsstraße 81-83](../../images/cc4bc6c7697b77451cb1e2c0633bfb3a_original.jpg)
*1922 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-29185 | Description: Forced labor company, Solmsstraße 81-83*

In 1943, the Bockenheim iron foundry and machine factory based at Solmsstrasse 81–83 made use of 10 forced labourers, 8 of whom were from France and 1 from Ukraine. It is not known where these persons were lodged.
