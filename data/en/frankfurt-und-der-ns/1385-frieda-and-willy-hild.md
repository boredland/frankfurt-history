# Frieda and Willy Hild

*Marbachweg 291*

![Willy Hild with Allemania cinema orchestra, 5th from left; L-000386](../../images/f5889f2d8c300ba9de5c241775ba16cb_original.jpg)
*License: Jehovas Zeugen, Archiv Zentraleuropa | Description: Willy Hild with Allemania cinema orchestra, 5th from left; L-000386*

For many years, Jehovah's Witnesses belonged to the "forgotten victims" and were hardly mentioned. We would like to bring the Frankfurt victims out of oblivion. Since 1997, the Frankfurt Jehovah's Witnesses who suffered under the Nazi regime because of their Christian convictions, have been remembered. Through volunteer work, archives and memorials are researching the experiences of the victims.

The research results and biographies are published on the website www.geschichte-jz-ffm.de.

By 2021, 30 Stolpersteine have been laid for this group of victims in Frankfurt.
