# NSDAP office Frankfurt

*Zeil 38*

![© Katharina Müller, VG Bild-Kunst, Bonn 2022](../../images/df5ce683e96f640e31681d4103d03239_original.jpg)
*2022 — Author: © Katharina Müller, VG Bild-Kunst, Bonn 2022 | Description: © Katharina Müller, VG Bild-Kunst, Bonn 2022*

From 1925, the National Socialist German Workers’ Party (NSDAP) had its first Frankfurt office on the second floor of Zeil 38, where it had rented a small room. As yet, the room did not have a typewriter and the furniture merely consisted of two lecterns from second-hand dealers.
