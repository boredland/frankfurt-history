# Stolen Place

*Fahrgasse 8*

![Fahrgasse 8](../../images/6d5922b4e8e692b6b475f9269426c0cc_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: Rainer Emanuel | License: h | Description: Fahrgasse 8*

With a purchase contract dated October 6, 1938, the city acquired the developed property at Klostergasse 4 with an area of 109 square meters from David Seidel, who had been persecuted for anti-Semitism, for the price of 6,500 Reichsmarks. The property was intended for the so-called Altstadtgesundung, i.e. gutting and new construction measures in the old town.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
