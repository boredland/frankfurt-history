# Stolen Place

*Zeil 80-82*

![Zeil 80-82](../../images/3aa289de5172614e6a8d76055d5803d8_original.jpg)
*ca. 1890 — Author: Rembrandt van Rijn | License: HMF | Description: Zeil 80-82*

The property at Zeil 80-82 originally belonged to Ludwig and Adolf Beckhardt. The anti-Semitic persecutees had to sell their house in 1939 to the company H. Kiefer & Co. KG, which had non-Jewish owners. The company had also acquired the house at Zeil 84 from Jewish owners.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
