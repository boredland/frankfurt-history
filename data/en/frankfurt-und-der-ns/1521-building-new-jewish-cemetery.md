# Building New Jewish cemetery

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 238*

![](../../images/f562aae7c76a87e6786fd91161d84184_original.jpg)
*2023*

In 1942, at least 15 people lived in the outbuilding of the New Jewish Cemetery. They were deported in September 1942. Their names were Recha Lehmann; Hilde and Leopold Levi; Elsa, Emil and Josef Fiebermann; Frieda and Albert Frohnhausen with their daughter Chana; Anna and Matthäus Oberbrunner with their son Rolf. The fate of Wolf Markovic is unclear. His son Rolf was deported. Bernhard Mayer, who moved to Liebigstraße and was then deported to Majdanek, also lived in the house. None of them survived! What brought these people together here and where did they work?

There are three Stumbling Stones for Else, Josef and Walter Fiebermann in the driveway of the house.
