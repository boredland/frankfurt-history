# Stolen Place

*Wiesenau 50*

![Wiesenau 50](../../images/82fdb8e45208e9d3ad386c3cb99b7176_original.jpg)
*before 1950 — Author: Postkartensammlung | License: ISG | Description: Wiesenau 50*

The property at Wiesenau 50 originally belonged to Lina Kohlmeier, née Goldmeier. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
