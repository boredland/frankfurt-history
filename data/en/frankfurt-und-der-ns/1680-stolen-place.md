# Stolen Place

*Herderstraße 30*

![Herderstraße 30](../../images/5eef734e496db40f943b4c74d9e46550_original.jpg)
*2010 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Herderstraße 30*

The property at Herderstraße 30 had originally belonged to Simon Sulzbacher. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
