# Stolen Place

*Oeder Weg 100*

![Oeder Weg 100](../../images/2d5e955f54137e986b070bac737b5260_original.jpg)
*2023 — Author: Hermann Tertilt | License: HMF | Description: Oeder Weg 100*

The property at Oeder Weg 100 originally belonged to Adolf Guckenheimer and his wife Settchen, née Hochstädter. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1940. The house was completely destroyed during the war. Hermann Tertilt researched the history of the former owner family in 2022 / 23 and established contact with the family's descendants in Chicago.
