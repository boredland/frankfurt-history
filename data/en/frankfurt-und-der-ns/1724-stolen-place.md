# Stolen Place

*Mainzer Landstraße 249*

![Mainzer Landstraße 249](../../images/067d6e87447edba99985fbe2542b90fb_original.jpg)
*1910 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Mainzer Landstraße 249*

The property at Mainzer Landstraße 249 originally belonged to a Jewish owner who had to sell it due to persecution. Later, the shoe factories J. & C.A. Schneider operated a camp for civilian workers at Mainzer Landstraße 249-251.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
