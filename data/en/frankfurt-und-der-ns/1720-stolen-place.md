# Stolen Place

*Liebigstraße 53*

![Liebigstraße 53](../../images/898e0d016795d76734b067b93dac6fc1_original.jpg)
*ca. 1890 — Author: Hertel | License: ISG | Description: Liebigstraße 53*

The property at Liebigstraße 53 had originally belonged to Helene Metz, née Erlanger, Dora Sern, née Metz, and Lotte Rosenstein, née Metz. The victims of anti-Semitic persecution were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
