# forced labour camp

*Eschborner Landstraße 172*

![Forced labor camp, Eschborner Landstraße 172](../../images/54c41472c580dd3510ed3ebc532a16ea_original.jpg)
*1935 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-30807 | Description: Forced labor camp, Eschborner Landstraße 172*

Two companies had their main headquarters at this location: the Westdeutsche Ziegelwerke brickworks and the Otto Graf & Co. vacuum and piston manufacturer. Both exploited forced labourers who were also housed here. In 1943, the Westdeutsche Ziegelwerke made use of 9 forced labourers from Ukraine, 6 from Poland, and 19 from France. The French workers were prisoners of war sent here by the Wehrmacht (the German army under National Socialism) from the Stalag IX B Wegscheide POW camp.
