# Residence of Margarete Wagner and Ignaz Hain

*Eppsteiner Straße 11*

![](../../images/ef288bf8953983d5813398031bcee12d_original.jpg)

Eppsteiner Straße 11 is the last known address of Margarete Wagner. She was the fiancée of Ignaz Hain. She was Catholic, he was Jewish. Their relationship was criminalized in 1935 by the Nuremberg Race Laws and they were forced to flee. Shortly before the end of the war in 1945, they both died separately: Margarete in a hospital in Civitella del Tronto in Italy and Ignaz Hain in the Mauthausen concentration camp.
A rediscovered suitcase tells the story of their lives. You can find out more in the biography below.
