# forced labour camp

*Berger Straße 161*

![Forced labour camp, Berger Straße 161](../../images/99c9916e324812b6cb66f61659ef77e4_original.jpg)
*1963 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2007-529 | Description: Forced labour camp, Berger Straße 161*

A jointly used camp for forced labourers reportedly existed here, in the former Schauburg cinema, from February 1944 to March 1945. In response to an inquiry from the Grüne (Green party) in 1983, the Hessian state government reported that 300 to 400 men were lodged here. It has not been possible to corroborate this on the basis of more recent research. Yet because there were six camps in Berger Strasse alone, the addresses may have been confused. No detailed information about this camp has yet been found.
