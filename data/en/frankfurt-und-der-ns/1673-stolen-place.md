# Stolen Place

*Hanauer Landstraße 17*

![Hanauer Landstraße 17](../../images/4c1f32a314b2c5209222bd32e4268fe2_original.jpg)
*2014 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Hanauer Landstraße 17*

With a purchase agreement dated April 3, 1939, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 502 square meters at Hanauer Landstraße 17 from the Suppenanstalt für Israelitische Arme e.V. association for the price of 40,000 Reichsmarks. The property was to go to the welfare office.
