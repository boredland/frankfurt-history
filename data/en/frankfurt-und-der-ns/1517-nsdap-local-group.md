# NSDAP local group

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 308*

![](../../images/10a69a43b9a929c6ec3c30b1f6ba2fb7_original.jpg)

This building was the headquarters of the local branch of the National Socialist German Workers' Party (NSDAP). The NSDAP had been in power in the German Reich since 1933. It also provided the Lord Mayor in Frankfurt. Its basic position was anti-Semitic, racist and anti-democratic. The local groups organized the party's work with local citizens.
