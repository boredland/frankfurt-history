# SA torture site

*Bockenheimer Anlage   15*

![Placeholder image](../../images/Hier fehlt_dfdc6d631f271e842082099187aa5623_original.jpg)
*License: HMF*

This was the location of the Masonic Lodge “Carl zur aufgehenden Sonne” (“Carl of the Rising Sun”), founded in 1816. Freemasons were also persecuted by the Nazi regime. In 1934, the Masonic Lodge was confiscated by the Gestapo and the SS (“Schutzstaffel”). The SA (“Sturmabteilung”), the paramilitary wing of the Nazi Party, used the building to set up a torture site where political opponents were beaten and abused.
