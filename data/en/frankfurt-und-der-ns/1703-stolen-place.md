# Stolen Place

*Königsteiner Straße 3c*

![Königsteiner Straße 3c](../../images/dc1a3062572ddb7c58954d5176aa2ce1_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Königsteiner Straße 3c*

The property at Königsteiner Straße 3c originally belonged to Hermann Hammerschlag. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and a non-Jewish couple in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
