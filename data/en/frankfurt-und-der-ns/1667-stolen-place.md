# Stolen Place

*Günthersburgallee 43*

![Günthersburgallee 43](../../images/a525e38d4c14a9eaacf8d49cde2b8fae_original.jpg)
*2011 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Günthersburgallee 43*

The property at Günthersburgallee 43 had originally belonged to Arthur Mayerfeld and his wife Ida, née Becker. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
