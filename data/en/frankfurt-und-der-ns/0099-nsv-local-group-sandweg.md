# NSV local group Sandweg

*Friedberger Anlage 18*

![Photo of the offices ofthe local groups Sandweg](../../images/4af2649e806c8afb472b4ff59c5ce95f_original.jpg)
*ca. 1940 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-10822 | Description: Photo of the offices ofthe local groups Sandweg*

A glimpse inside the 1935 directory shows that altogether six local chapters of National Socialist organizations had their headquarters here: the NS German Workers’ Party, the NS Women’s League, the NS People’s Welfare, the NS Crafts, Trade, and Industry Organization, the NS Factory Cell Organization, and the German Labour Front. These organizations and others like them transported the Nazi ideology into every area of family life. They supplanted the many charitable, social, and political initiatives that had mirrored and represented citizens’ needs and interests during the Weimar Republic.
