# Local groups of NSDAP and NSDAP Frauenschaft Eschersheim

*Eschersheimer Landstraße 542*

![Photo of the offcies of the local groups of the NSDAP and NSDAP Women's League Eschersheim<span class="tab2"><br /></span>](../../images/97e290576eda1fe42747f5a77dbe602f_original.jpg)
*2002 — Author: Martin Starl | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2015-575 | Description: Photo of the offcies of the local groups of the NSDAP and NSDAP Women's League Eschersheim<span class="tab2"><br /></span>*

This was the location of the local Eschersheim branch of the National Socialist German Workers’ Party (NSDAP) and the associated National Socialist Women’s League. The local branches were subordinate to the district level. Each branch was responsible for some 1,500 households, and oversaw the cell and block leaders in their areas. A block leader was responsible for 40 to 60 households, which he kept watch over and spied on. The local branches not only gathered information on party members in their neighbourhoods, but also everyone living there. In this way, social control extended even into individual residential buildings. In Frankfurt, records show the city had 47 local branch leaders, 670 cell leaders, and 3,630 block leaders.
