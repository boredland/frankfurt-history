# forced labour camp

*Alt Fechenheim 123*

![Forced labor camp, Alt Fechenheim 123](../../images/47366bf8cc54190a86b53e696a8fcbec_original.jpg)
*ca. 1906 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-21465 | Description: Forced labor camp, Alt Fechenheim 123*

In 1944, 24 forced labourers from France were housed on the first floor of the Zum Schwan guesthouse. They worked for the model builder Bruno Bachmann, whose premises were a four-minute walk away at Fachfeldstrasse 27. Bachmann had requested 26 additional forced labourers. In 1946, the 7th police district reported 18 forced labourers from France to the United Nations Relief and Rehabilitation Administration (UNRRA). The UNRRA was in charge of registering persons from the member states of the U.N., looking after them, and helping them return to their home countries.
