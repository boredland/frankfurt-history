# Stolen Place

*Westendstraße 52*

![Westendstraße 52](../../images/a7f0b1945ef85e23d8c54e1dddbb3e85_original.jpg)
*1941 — Author: Leuschner | License: ISG | Description: Westendstraße 52*

The property at Westendstraße 52 originally belonged to Ferdinand Hirsch and his wife Anna Pauline, née Mayer. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer during the Nazi era.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
