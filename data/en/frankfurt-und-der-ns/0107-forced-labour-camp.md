# Forced labour camp

*Mainzer Landstraße 134-142*

![Schloßstraße](../../images/55bfacfbd8ed5fd919360f87019dbb8c_original.jpg)
*before 1911 — Author: Hermann Bückling | License: HMF, C20438a | Description: Schloßstraße*

The Telefonbau & Normalzeit telecommunications company made use of forced labourers from Eastern Europe in its operations. They had their workplaces either at the factory grounds at Mainzer Landstrasse 134–142 or at Kleyerstrasse 79–87, where they were presumably also housed. The sources document 53 forced labourers from Lithuania and 66 from Russia for the year 1942. In 1943, some 230 forced labourers from Eastern Europe worked for the company.
