NSV local group University

The NSV's local university group had its headquarters at Universitätsstraße 21.
The address Mertonstraße (today renamed again) was renamed Universitätsstraße in 1938 in order to erase the memory of the Merton family, who had Jewish roots and had been instrumental in founding the university.
