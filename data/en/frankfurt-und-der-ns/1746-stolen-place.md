# Stolen Place

*Pfingstweidstraße 14*

![Pfingstweidstraße 14](../../images/12990144b00c564a8966ecbc8f141dd6_original.jpg)
*2014 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Pfingstweidstraße 14*

The property at Pfingstweidstraße 14 had originally belonged to Johanna Schön, née Freimarck. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
