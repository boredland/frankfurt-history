# Stolen Place

*Münchener Straße 9*

![Münchener Straße 9](../../images/5b0d4d9fed9fd85303bab9f958c3fd23_original.jpg)
*1980 — Author: Falk Kynast | License: ISG | Description: Münchener Straße 9*

The property at Kronprinzenstraße 9 (now Münchener Straße) had originally belonged to Josef Bergmann and his wife Anna, née Gradus. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
