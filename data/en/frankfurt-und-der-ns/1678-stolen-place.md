# Stolen Place

*Hausener Obergasse 49*

![Hausener Obergasse 49](../../images/a15ce7670c908fc001b3d00e79698266_original.jpg)
*1934 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Hausener Obergasse 49*

The property at Hausener Landstraße 112 (today Hausener Obergasse 49-49h) originally belonged to Helene Baer, née Kaufmann, and had to be sold to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939. Later there was a camp for civilian workers here.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
