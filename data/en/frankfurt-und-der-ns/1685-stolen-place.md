# Stolen Place

*Homburger Landstraße 152*

![Homburger Landstraße 152](../../images/6e2c2fb43415eca008a3db8fa2065381_original.jpg)
*2017 — Author: Claus Graubner | License: ISG | Description: Homburger Landstraße 152*

The property at Homburger Landstraße 152 had originally belonged to Salomon Hochschild. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
