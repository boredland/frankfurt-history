# Stolen Place

*Zeil 43*

![Zeil 43](../../images/5dab38759c1c44186c97e6ffaee8c68b_original.jpg)
*n. d. — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Zeil 43*

The property at Zeil 43 / Albusstraße 38 originally belonged to Joseph Nussbaum. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
