# Forced labour camp

*Schmickstraße 63*

![Aerial view of Schmickstraße 63, where Gebr. Haldy Kohlenhandelsgesellschaft GmbH operated a forced labour camp](../../images/d7f03f8750836057ea384b619017a356_original.jpg)
