# Stolen Place

*Stettenstraße 9*

![Stettenstraße 9](../../images/ea78c7bcabd05f187f83c40e27a6ffbb_original.jpg)
*ca. 1923 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Stettenstraße 9*

The property at Stettenstraße 9 originally belonged to Kurt Mohr. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
