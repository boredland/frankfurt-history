# Stolen Place

*Schwindstraße 5*

![Schwindstraße 5](../../images/ee49f4a87c312a44afa142c25d787ad6_original.jpg)
*1878 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Schwindstraße 5*

The property at Schwindstraße 5 originally belonged to Raphael and Rudolf Mastbaum. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
