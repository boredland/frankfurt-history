# Stolen Place

*Westendstraße 28*

![Westendstraße 28](../../images/f7498f1b621b2439bfe93517f43c2cfa_original.jpg)
*before 1950 — Author: Postkartensammlung | License: ISG | Description: Westendstraße 28*

The property at Westendstraße 28 originally belonged to Dr. Arthur Oppenheimer and Selig Nussbaum. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to non-Jewish buyers in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
