# Stolen Place

*Neue Mainzer Straße 24*

![Neue Mainzer Straße 24](../../images/a33428e29e27e46bc483480c1d5571ba_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Neue Mainzer Straße 24*

The property at Neue Mainzer Straße 24 had belonged to Jewish owners who had to sell it due to persecution. It later housed a camp for civilian workers from the company Glasbau Georg Schäfer & Co.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
