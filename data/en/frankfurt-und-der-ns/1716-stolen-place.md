# Stolen Place

*Leipziger Straße 1*

![Leipziger Straße 1](../../images/93d0432e2cc691e7e33d886c3f499c84_original.jpg)
*before 1900 — Author: Hermann Bückling | License: ISG | Description: Leipziger Straße 1*

The property at Leipziger Straße 1 had originally belonged to Max Fisch. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938. As this sale was not approved, he had to sell it again in 1939 to a non-Jewish couple.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
