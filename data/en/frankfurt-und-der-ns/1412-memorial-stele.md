# Memorial stele

*Rohrbachstraße 58*

![<p> Memorial stele at Rohrbachstraße 58</p>](../../images/88bb76965cbdd0cf4c6f95b2bd6f0a6a_original.jpg)
*License: Erika und Günter Krämer | Description: <p> Memorial stele at Rohrbachstraße 58</p>*

In memory of the baker Martin Bertram and all other Jehovah's Witnesses in Frankfurt who bravely defied the Nazi regime, a memorial stele was erected at Rohrbachstrasse 58 by the Cultural Department of the City of Frankfurt to commemorate this extraordinary steadfastness. On 5 September 2005, Dr Hans-Bernhard Nordhoff, Head of the Cultural Department of the City of Frankfurt, and Josef Niklasch, the last concentration camp survivor from the group of Jehovah's Witness victims in Frankfurt, jointly unveiled this memorial stele.
