# forced labour camp

*Friedberger Landstraße 298*

![Forced labor company, Friedberger Landstraße 298](../../images/a98da970cec9326f1379f2c77a634ea8_original.jpg)
*2000 — Author: Martin Starl | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2014-759 | Description: Forced labor company, Friedberger Landstraße 298*

The A. von Kaick generator factory based at Friedberger Landstrasse 298 profited from forced labour. In 1944, 16 Ukrainian women and 42 Ukrainian men as well as 6 French men were registered as residing on the grounds of the company headquarters.
