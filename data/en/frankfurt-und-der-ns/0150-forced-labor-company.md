# forced labor company

*Mainzer Landstraße 249-251*

![Forced labor company, Mainzer Landstraße 249-251](../../images/9f14e714ac554e2001fa539fbbb6dac7_original.jpg)
*1910 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-13199 | Description: Forced labor company, Mainzer Landstraße 249-251*

The J. & C. A. Schneider shoe factories were based on premises that the Lindheimers, a family of industrialists, had been forced to sell in 1939 because they were Jewish. During World War II, J. & C. A. Schneider operated two camps in Frankfurt. Some 100 forced labourers were exploited at Mainzer Landstrasse 249–251 and 281–291. They were housed at Kostheimer Strasse 9 and Alt-Praunheim 42–44. The majority of them were from Belgium.
