# Stolen Place

*Große Bockenheimer Straße 33*

![Große Bockenheimer Straße 33](../../images/5426899de0d212ec57850c1cb66e2b74_original.jpg)
*ca. 1910 — Author: L. Klaus | License: ISG | Description: Große Bockenheimer Straße 33*

The property at Große Bockenheimer Straße 33 had originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
