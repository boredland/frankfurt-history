# Stolen Place

*Bornheimer Landwehr 77*

![Bornheimer Landwehr 77](../../images/ce6ffc6562050086be97491e007ee1bd_original.jpg)
*License: ISG | Description: Bornheimer Landwehr 77*

With a purchase contract dated April 14, 1939, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 2,607 square meters, which belonged to the former Jewish hospital, from the Frankfurt Association of Jewish Nurses for 142,000 Reichsmark. The property was to go to the welfare office.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
