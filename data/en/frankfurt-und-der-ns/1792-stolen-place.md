# Stolen Place

*Ziegelgasse 20*

![Ziegelgasse 20](../../images/9c3a450c4a82257fa358ad71145b9973_original.jpg)
*1939 — Author: Fried Lübbecke | License: ISG | Description: Ziegelgasse 20*

In a forced auction on December 14, 1940, the city acquired the developed properties at Ziegelgasse 7-9 / Neue Kräme 30 for the price of 53,000 Reichsmark. The property covered a total area of 525 square meters. The houses belonged to Otto and Hugo Brumlik. The property was earmarked for the so-called Altstadtgesundung, i.e. gutting and new construction measures in the old town, as well as for traffic improvements.
