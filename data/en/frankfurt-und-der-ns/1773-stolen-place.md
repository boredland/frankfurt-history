# Stolen Place

*Vilbeler Straße 3*

![Vilbeler Straße 3](../../images/6c17dbc2cf49acba8f8f033ed468db49_original.jpg)
*n. d. — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Vilbeler Straße 3*

The property at Vilbeler Straße 3 originally belonged to Max Fisch. There was a café on the first floor. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
