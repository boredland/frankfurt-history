NS-Reichskriegerbund “Kyffhäuser”

The Nazi Reichskriegerbund “Kyffhäuser” had its headquarters at Niedenau 61.
Since March 4, 1938 the Nazi Reichskriegerbund “Kyffhäuser”. On March 3, 1943, Adolf Hitler dissolved the Kyffhäuserbund at Reich level. Its assets were transferred to the NSDAP and the remaining local associations, which formed the basis for the Volkssturm units in the final phase of the Second World War, were placed under the control of the party.
