# Bomb shelter

*Karlstraße 4-6*

![Photo of a bomb shelter located in Karlstraße](../../images/8a6c2fe7e1ce83b2b4386ef4e70b0e63_original.jpg)
*n.d. — Author: unbekannt | License: Denkmalamt Stadt Frankfurt | Description: Photo of a bomb shelter located in Karlstraße*

This bomb shelter offered air-raid protection to the residents of the station district. Little is known about it, however, as no records of the construction project have come down to us. It comprised three floors, one below and two above ground level. The bomb shelters of Frankfurt provided space for a total of only 35,000 residents. Others sought refuge in the cellars of their houses or in public air-raid cellars, which were not bombproof. When word of this got out, more and more people crowded into the bomb shelters. Jews, forced labourers, and prisoners of war, however, were prohibited from entering. The office building you see here was built on top of the shelter. The World War II structure is concealed behind the façade of the ground floor.
