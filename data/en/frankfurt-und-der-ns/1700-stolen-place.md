# Stolen Place

*Kettenhofweg 94*

![Kettenhofweg 94](../../images/6f888d72968c3da2edbd58942452c6a0_original.jpg)
*ca. 1990 — Author: Gebrüder Seeger | License: ISG | Description: Kettenhofweg 94*

The property at Kettenhofweg 94 had originally belonged to Leo Kahn and his wife Emma, née Hiller. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
