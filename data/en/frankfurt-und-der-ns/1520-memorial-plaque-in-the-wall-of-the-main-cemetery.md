# Memorial plaque in the wall of the main cemetery

*Eckenheimer Landstraße*

![](../../images/d9fdd856314aa70cda36361425c01efd_original.jpg)

There is a memorial plaque on the wall of the main cemetery. It commemorates the fact that this wall was built in 1939 using stones from destroyed synagogues in Frankfurt. During the November pogroms of 1938, synagogues, i.e. Jewish places of worship, were looted and set on fire by German "Volksgenossen". The city council forced the Jewish community to pay for the demolition of the ruins. The stones were sold to a construction company, which used them to build the cemetery wall.
