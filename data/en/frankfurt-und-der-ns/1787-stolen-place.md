# Stolen Place

*Zeil 72-78*

![Zeil 72-78](../../images/d9098ec9a0bf360ea990ca60a93bb369_original.jpg)
*1939 — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Zeil 72-78*

The property at Zeil 72-78 originally belonged to Ludwig Beckhardt. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his business premises to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938. The property, which was restituted after 1945, was sold by Ludwig Beckhardt's heir to Alois Ammerschläger in 1954.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
