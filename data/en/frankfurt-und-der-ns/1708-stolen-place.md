# Stolen Place

*Kurfürstenstraße 20*

![Kurfürstenstraße 20](../../images/ae8d6a16a5eb6a5a03b36bd4f6e946f1_original.jpg)
*1906 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kurfürstenstraße 20*

The property at Kurfürstenstraße 20 had originally belonged to Albert Straus and his wife Melina, née Strauss. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
