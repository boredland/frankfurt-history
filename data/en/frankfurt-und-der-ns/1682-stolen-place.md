# Stolen Place

*Holbeinstraße 29*

![Holbeinstraße 29](../../images/3298f2e42a2bc3519b1df0edd3f63c97_original.jpg)
*ca. 1908 — Author: Gustav Günther | License: ISG | Description: Holbeinstraße 29*

The property at Holbeinstraße 28 had originally belonged to David Gross and his wife Berta, née Rothschild. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
