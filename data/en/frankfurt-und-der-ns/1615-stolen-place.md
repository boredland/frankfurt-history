# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 104*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 104](../../images/f12df0ab163f631ff02d740104c529ce_original.jpg)
*ca. 1937 — License: ISG | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 104*

With a purchase agreement dated August 3, 1936, the city acquired the developed property of 6,139 square meters from Robert von Hirsch. The property was to be used for the expansion of the university. Instead of a purchase price, the owners received building plots on Dielmannstraße and Habsburger Allee in exchange.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
