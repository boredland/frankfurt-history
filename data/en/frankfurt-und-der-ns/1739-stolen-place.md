# Stolen Place

*Oberlindau 1*

![Oberlindau 1](../../images/3a9bff7c33e6f14dc18c0d833a961b19_original.jpg)
*n. d. — Author: unbekannt | License: HMF | Description: Oberlindau 1*

With a purchase agreement dated January 10, 1939, the city acquired the developed property Oberlindau 1 / Bockenheimer Landstraße 18 with an area of 1,659 square meters from Abraham Horowitz and his wife Rosi, née Feist, who had been under anti-Semitic persecution, for the price of 43,000 Reichsmarks. The property was intended for administrative purposes.
