# Stolen Place

*Kettenhofweg 27*

![Kettenhofweg 27](../../images/81f07d56a42f52a05e5c90f51e523442_original.jpg)
*1880 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kettenhofweg 27*

The property at Kettenhofweg 27 had originally belonged to Emma Merton, née Ladenburg. She was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell her house to a non-Jewish aristocratic buyer in 1938. The building had been converted from a commercial to a residential building in the mid-1929s.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
