*Meisengasse 10*

This was the site of the H. & K. Vetter home decoration and furniture store. During the war, forced and civilian workers were deployed here. They were housed in assembly camps, either at Allerheiligenstraße 26 or Rückertstraße 53. The records show a French forced laborer working here in 1943. Dimitry Szyló similarly was made to work here.
