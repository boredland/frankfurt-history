# forced labour camp

*Neue Mainzer Straße 24*

![Neue Mainzer Straße 24](../../images/7e8afdbf352e4be5c04afcc61262d70a_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 2014-126 | Description: Neue Mainzer Straße 24*

The company Glasbau Georg Schäfer &Co. operated a camp for forced laborers at Neue Mainzer Straße 24.
The property had originally belonged to a Jewish owner who had to sell it due to persecution.
