# forced labour camp

*Landgrafenstraße 12*

![Forced labor camp, Landgrafenstraße 12](../../images/61c640dcb86cbedb24bec44f34247588_original.jpg)
*1907 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-20090 | Description: Forced labor camp, Landgrafenstraße 12*

Josef Münstermann & Co., a foodstuffs and egg wholesale company based at Landgrafenstrasse 12, made use of forced labourers. In 1943, 3 Eastern Europeans of unknown nationality were made to work for the company. They had their lodgings in a jointly used camp at Marburger Strasse 28.
