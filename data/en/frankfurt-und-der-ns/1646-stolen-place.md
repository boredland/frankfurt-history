# Stolen Place

*Feuerbachstraße 1*

![Feuerbachstraße 1](../../images/1e67a710cf19e548a03360eaad76bb32_original.jpg)
*before 1933 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Feuerbachstraße 1*

The property at Feuerbachstraße 1 originally belonged to the Jewish woman Beatrice Wilding, née Joseph. She had previously lived in Wolfgang and around 1943 was living in exile in the USA, of which she was a citizen. A court-appointed guardian in absentia was appointed for her remaining assets in Germany. The building comprised two 3-room apartments and one 4-room apartment. After the USA entered the war, the Reichskommissar für die Behandlung feindlichen Vermögens moved into the house and had it administered in favor of the Reich.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
