# Stolen Place

*An der Staufenmauer 5*

![An der Staufenmauer 5](../../images/ab312aa551a45bc7c8fc87bed9407cb4_original.jpg)
*Author: Kuropatwa | License: ISG | Description: An der Staufenmauer 5*

The property at Großer Wollgraben 35 (today An der Saufenmauer 5) originally belonged to Hedwig Groedel, née Drissler. Persecuted for anti-Semitism, she was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
