# Stolen Place

*Leipziger Straße 51*

![Leipziger Straße 51](../../images/b2e6011ddd77c14dbd99c26c24b78d6e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Leipziger Straße 51*

The properties at Leipziger Strasse 51 and Kurfürstenstrasse 1 had originally belonged to Dr. Arthur Nawartzki. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his houses to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
