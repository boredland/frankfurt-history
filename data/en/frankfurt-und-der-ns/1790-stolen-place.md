# Stolen Place

*Zeil 92*

![Zeil 92](../../images/1df6bb0516ec526818189938d7c3f5d0_original.jpg)
*before 1944 — Author: Leonhard Kleemann | License: HMF | Description: Zeil 92*

Baron Wilhelm and Baroness Mathilde von Rothschild's old people's home for Israelite women and maidens of the upper classes was originally located at Zeil 92. It was forcibly transferred to the Reich Association of Jews in Germany. In a contract dated November 30, 1942, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 948 square meters from the Reich Association of Jews in Germany, in which the Jewish Community and the Jewish Religious Community had been forcibly united, for the price of 300,000 Reichsmark. The property was to be used for administrative purposes of the tramway and preserved as an architectural monument.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
