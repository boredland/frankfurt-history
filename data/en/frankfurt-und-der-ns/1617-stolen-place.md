# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 118*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 118 and 120](../../images/f81e6a5e6345af31b759e34b3612a823_original.jpg)
*ca. 1909 — License: ISG | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 118 and 120*

With a purchase contract dated August 11, 1938, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 1,323 square meters from Mrs. M. Schliep for the price of 50,000 Reichsmark. The area was intended for a reorganization of the Palmengarten and was to be used for residential purposes until then.
