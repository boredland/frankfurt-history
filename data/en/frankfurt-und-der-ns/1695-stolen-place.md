# Stolen Place

*Kantstraße 6*

![Kantstraße 6](../../images/9ff89cad40bcc3fd021432227ed9291f_original.jpg)
*2012 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Kantstraße 6*

The property at Kantstraße 6 had originally belonged to Max Fisch. He was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
