# Stolen Place

*Holzhausenstraße 25*

![Holzhausenstraße 25](../../images/97088586c83630b4a7cb9df910a6bcd4_original.jpg)
*1983 — Author: Jutta Hofmann | License: ISG | Description: Holzhausenstraße 25*

The property at Holzhausenstraße 25 had originally belonged to Eugen Hess. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
