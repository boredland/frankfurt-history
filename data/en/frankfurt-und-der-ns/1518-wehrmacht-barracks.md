# Wehrmacht barracks

*Feuerwehrstraße 1*

![](../../images/c5297ca38428e969630fe61f6ff6ef4a_original.jpg)

The Wehrmacht barracks of an intelligence unit, inaugurated in 1938, were located here. After the Second World War, the barracks were used by the US Army. Today it houses the fire and disaster control center of the Frankfurt fire department. Some of the barracks buildings are empty.
