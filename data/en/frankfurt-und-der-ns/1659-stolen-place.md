# Stolen Place

*Goethestraße 4-8*

![Goethestraße 4-8](../../images/6a6a08adbecedcef18293c879ef38e3c_original.jpg)
*1904 — Author: Th. Hufnagel | License: ISG | Description: Goethestraße 4-8*

The property at Goethestrasse 4-8 originally belonged to Heinrich and Rudolf Lismann. The house of the anti-Semitic persecutees was put up for forced sale in 1940.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
