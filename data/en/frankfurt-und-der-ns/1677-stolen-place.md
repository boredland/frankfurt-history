# Stolen Place

*Hasengasse 15-17*

![Picture postcard as a receipt and advertising card for the company S.Wronker &amp; Co.](../../images/a60a5b1dd52555079a95c622cbf63059_original.jpg)
*1898 — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Picture postcard as a receipt and advertising card for the company S.Wronker &amp; Co.*

The property at Hasengasse 15-17 originally belonged to the department store owner Hermann Wronker. He was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
