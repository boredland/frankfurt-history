# forced labour camp

*Hainer Weg 24*

![Forced labor camp, Hainer Weg 24](../../images/e1ed997db25afcd9ebfd51b90159b961_original.jpg)
*ca. 1942 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 2013-9 | Description: Forced labor camp, Hainer Weg 24*

The Georg Klein dye works and chemical laundries company operated a forced labour camp at this site. In 1942, 14 forced labourers from Russia were lodged here. By 1943 the number had grown to 20 Russians and 6 forced labourers from Lithuania.
