# Stolen Place

*Marbachweg 318*

![Marbachweg 318](../../images/77054fe1e5e5454ff541d81bb0b6aec8_original.jpg)
*ca. 1928 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Marbachweg 318*

The property at Marbachweg 318 had originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
