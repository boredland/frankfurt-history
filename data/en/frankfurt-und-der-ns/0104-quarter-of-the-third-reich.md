# Quarter of the "Third Reich"

*Untermainkai 27*

![Architectural model of a construction project called ‘Quarter of the Third Reich’](../../images/7f19f3643dab19718dba685c79c7b7bd_original.jpg)
*1937 — Author: Hugo Schmölz | License: HMF C35090 | Description: Architectural model of a construction project called ‘Quarter of the Third Reich’*

In 1935, the German Labour Front planned a huge annex on the grounds of the trade union hall. It was part of a major construction project called the “District of the Third Reich” that was to be built on the northern bank of the Main. It was also to encompass a “House of German Crafts” and a “Gauhaus”, or regional party headquarters. Buildings of cultural and historical importance such as the union hall and the Rothschild Palais were to be demolished. The architect Clemens Klotz, who worked for the German Labour Front, drew up the plans. Ultimately, however, the project never got off the ground.
