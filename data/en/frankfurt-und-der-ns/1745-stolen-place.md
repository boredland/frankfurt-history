# Stolen Place

*Pfingstweidstraße 12*

![Pfingstweidstraße 12](../../images/787d0dc8c4befc602a66465cf67eb198_original.jpg)
*2012 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Pfingstweidstraße 12*

The property at Pfingstweidstraße 12 had originally belonged to Minna Plaut, née Bodenheimer. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1941.
