# forced labor company

*Schielestraße 18-36*

![Photo of the premises of Messer &amp; Co., where a barracks camp for forced labourers was operated](../../images/da795a031957adb5922c006fddcfdb7d_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-13989 | Description: Photo of the premises of Messer &amp; Co., where a barracks camp for forced labourers was operated*

On the premises of the Gaswerk Ost (east side gas works) located at this site, the Main Gaswerke utilities company made use of forced labourers who were lodged in barracks at the corner of Schielestrasse and Riederhofstrasse. In the autumn of 1942, 16 forced labourers from Russia and 10 from Lithuania lived in this camp, in 1943 31 from Russia and 8 from Lithuania. In 1944, 38 forced labourers from Russia, 3 from Lithuania, and 9 from France were registered here.
