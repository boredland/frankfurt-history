# Stolen Place

*Kurfürstenstraße 1*

![Kurfürstenstraße 1](../../images/b2e6011ddd77c14dbd99c26c24b78d6e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kurfürstenstraße 1*

The properties at Kurfürstenstraße 1 and Leipziger Straße 51 had originally belonged to Dr. Arthur Nawartzki. He was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell his houses to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
