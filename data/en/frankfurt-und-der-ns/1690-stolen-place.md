# Stolen Place

*Kaiserstraße 4*

![Kaiserstraße 4](../../images/4e737ab6c326f7dd960e4eebfd9c5639_original.jpg)
*ca. 1909 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kaiserstraße 4*

The property at Kaiserstrasse 4 had originally belonged to Jewish owners. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
