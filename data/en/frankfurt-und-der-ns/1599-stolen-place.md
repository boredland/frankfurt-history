# Stolen Place

*Alte Falterstraße 32*

![Alte Falterstraße 32](../../images/6dfc00cefd9d61b8f832f07209e10350_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — License: ISG | Description: Alte Falterstraße 32*

With a purchase agreement dated December 1, 1938, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 135 square meters from the merchant H. Hammel and his wife for the price of 18,000 Reichsmark. It was sold to the Stadtsparkasse, which also had a savings bank there.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
