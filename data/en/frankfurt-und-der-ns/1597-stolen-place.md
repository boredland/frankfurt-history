# Stolen Place

*Allerheiligenstraße 30*

![Allerheiligenstraße 30](../../images/aed68550f217fef0dc11ce0656bce0da_original.jpg)
*1955 — License: ISG | Description: Allerheiligenstraße 30*

The four-storey tenement and commercial building at Allerheiligengasse 30 with two stores and ten apartments originally belonged to Siegfried and Hermann Merzbacher. The Jewish owners were expatriated by the Reich Minister of the Interior on October 31, 1939 and their property was declared forfeited to the Reich. The Berlin Moabit-West tax office responsible for property management intended to sell the property in April 1941. The building authority of the city of Frankfurt set the purchase price at up to 40,000 Reichmarks and also submitted a purchase offer on January 24, 1942 to acquire the land for the widening of Breite Gasse.
