# Stolen Place

*Kaiserstraße 41*

![Kaiserstraße 41](../../images/6ff15f971cfd9b966bf20e6f859568e7_original.jpg)
*ca. 1947 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Kaiserstraße 41*

The property at Kaiserstrasse 41 had originally belonged to Georg Freudensten, Siegmund and Gerson Neuberger. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their property in 1940 to a non-Jewish buyer and a non-Jewish couple.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
