# Stolen Place

*Merianstraße 25*

![Merianstraße 25](../../images/d3d5b3208b4a27f6cb004b0b008ef551_original.jpg)
*2010 — Author: Martin Starl | License: ISG | Description: Merianstraße 25*

The property at Merianstraße 25 had originally belonged to Ottilie Waldmann. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
