# forced labour camp

*Gotenstraße 6*

![Forced labor camp, Gotenstraße 6](../../images/994dc2a28fd15c33d0cb2f61d7b6f2b1_original.jpg)
*before 1941 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S17 Nr. 847 | Description: Forced labor camp, Gotenstraße 6*

The City of Frankfurt am Main operated a camp for forced labourers in the municipal hospital at Gotenstrasse 6. No information has come down to us about how many persons lived here and where they came from.
