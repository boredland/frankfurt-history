# NSDAP district management Groß-Frankfurt

*Elbestraße 1*

![Photo of the offices of the National Socialist Organization for Crafts, Trade, and Commerce (NS Hago) and District Office Management at Elbestraße 1](../../images/c992dec756cf2e004e7198e57aa5ea5f_original.jpg)
*1934 — Author: Hannah Reeck | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-11200 | Description: Photo of the offices of the National Socialist Organization for Crafts, Trade, and Commerce (NS Hago) and District Office Management at Elbestraße 1*

The district leadership of the National Socialist German Workers’ Party and the National Socialist Peoples’ Welfare had their offices here. From 1933 to 1937, the district leader was Friedrich Krebs. The regional leader Jakob Sprenger had appointed him to this office, and on 13 March 1933 also nominated him for the office of interim mayor. In June, Krebs officially took over the position of mayor, which he held until March 1945. The German Labour Front had various offices on the second floor: the district administration, the district factory cell leadership, and the National Socialist “Strength Through Joy” Society. The district leadership of the National Socialist Crafts, Trade, and Industry Organization, which in 1935 was merged with the German Labour Front’s Reich Works Community of Crafts and Trade, also had its offices here.
