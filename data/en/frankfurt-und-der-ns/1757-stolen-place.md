# Stolen Place

*Schubertstraße 1*

![Schubertstraße 1](../../images/ad0a00a05c4226ea4ea9377b8cb2fb4e_original.jpg)
*ca. 1910 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG, S7A Nr. 1998-13242 | Description: Schubertstraße 1*

The property at Schubertstraße 1 originally belonged to Rudolph Kahn. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to two non-Jewish buyers in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
