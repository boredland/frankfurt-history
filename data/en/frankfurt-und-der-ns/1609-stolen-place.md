# Stolen Place

*Bethmannstraße 32*

![Bethmannstraße 32](../../images/3dfacd962590013345d30d4c7483ccee_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — License: ISG | Description: Bethmannstraße 32*

The property at Bethmannstraße 32 originally belonged to Isidor van Elkan and his wife Lina van Elkan, née Levy, Baumweg 38. Due to persecution, they had to sell the three-storey apartment building with a plot area of 137 square meters to the city for the price of 17,000 Reichsmarks in a purchase contract dated 10 November 1938. The property was intended for the so-called Altstadtgesundung, i.e. gutting and new construction measures in the old town.
