# House of the Schwarzschild family and Richard Ohlmann

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 307*

![](../../images/14bf66afad20bd298010569c9eab6b8f_original.jpg)
*2023*

Josef and Selma Schwarzschild lived here. They had to move from here to Uhlandstraße. They were deported from there on September 15, 1942. They died in Theresienstadt in 1943.

Richard Ohlmann also lived in the same house. He was deported to Theresienstadt in February 1945, where he died three weeks after liberation on May 29, 1945.
