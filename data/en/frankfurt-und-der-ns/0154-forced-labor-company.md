# forced labor company

*Voltastraße 74-80*

![Forced labor company, Voltastraße 74-80](../../images/1aa05ca8d8b3b5793be58dc5ef91ec32_original.jpg)
*unknown — Author: unbekannt | License: HMF, Ph13640 | Description: Forced labor company, Voltastraße 74-80*

At this site, the Moenus machine factory made use of forced labourers who were lodged nearby at Solmsstrasse 35. In 1943, they numbered 30 from Russia and 20 from Ukraine. The camp was in existence from 1942 to 1945.
