# Stolen Place

*Eckenheimer Landstraße 238*

![Eckenheimer Landstraße 238](../../images/3fd8a0568736d6176f2e490e45be0180_original.jpg)
*1929 — Author: Hermann Collischonn | License: HMF | Description: Eckenheimer Landstraße 238*

The Jewish cemetery on Eckenheimer Landstraße, inaugurated in 1928, had to be forcibly sold to the city of Frankfurt in March 1939, along with many other community properties such as the synagogues, the Jewish hospital and the Philantrophin. Around 800 people who had taken their own lives out of despair in the face of persecution and the threat of deportation found their final resting place in the cemetery. The Jewish community erected a memorial for the victims of the Shoah here in 1959.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
