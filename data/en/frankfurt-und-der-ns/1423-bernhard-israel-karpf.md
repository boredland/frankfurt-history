# Bernhard Israel Karpf

*5 Places – 5 Stories*

![](../../images/2ea3f2dbb6f77c3c19ae94d6a2fa84cf_original.jpg)

Bernhard Karpf, who was born on 04.09.2023, was a German Jew who lived together with his wife Bertha Karpf at Sandweg 29 in Frankfurt. Until December 1938, Mr. Karpf pursued his work with substantial restrictions, which brought him an annual income of 3800 Reichsmark, well above average. 
From 1939/40 on, he lived on welfare. This also financed his emigration to the United States, which he applied for in 1939 and started in 1940. 
For his house and other parts of the property, he had to sign a waiver. Due to his financial hardship, the foreign exchange office had to reduce his dego fees.

After the departure, his traces were lost.
