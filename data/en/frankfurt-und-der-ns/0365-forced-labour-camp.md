# Forced labour camp

*Hanauer Landstraße 52*

![Placeholder image](../../images/Hier fehlt_dfdc6d631f271e842082099187aa5623_original.jpg)
*License: HMF*

The Georg von Olnhausen company, a manufacturer of blankets, tents, and awnings, was based at Hanauer Landstrasse 52. It made use of forced labourers, some of whom also had their lodgings here. In 1942, between 20 and 52 Russian women had to perform forced labour here. They lived in rooms on the upper level of the factory building. Sinti and Roma from the internment camp in Dieselstrasse were also exploited here. The camp was in existence until 1944.
