# forced labour camp

*Kurt-Schumacher-Straße 15-17*

![Forced labor camp, Kurt-Schumacher-Straße 15-17](../../images/Ph06493_jp_71d707ec2322c5105412c41f0214393f_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: Rainer Emanuel | License: HMF, Ph06493 | Description: Forced labor camp, Kurt-Schumacher-Straße 15-17*

Klostergasse 8 (now Kurt-Schumacher-Strasse) was the site of a forced labour camp. It was operated by the Alfred Teves machine and fixtures factory, which lodged more than 1,500 forced labourers in several camps.
