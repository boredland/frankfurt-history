# Stolen Place

*Reuterweg 49*

![Reuterweg 49](../../images/2a4050f0c0d312279c9cb3c42b847310_original.jpg)
*1939 — Author: Georg Krauss | License: ISG | Description: Reuterweg 49*

The property at Reuterweg 49 / corner of Kronberger Straße 1 originally belonged to Dr. Alfred Jacob. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
