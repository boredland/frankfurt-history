# Home of Maria and Karl Geist

*Wittelsbacherallee 143*

![](../../images/9e829bc222b70178f8a2264af7383207_original.png)
*License: Google Street View*

Maria Geist (1898-1975) lived here with her husband Karl Geist (1900-1982) on the first floor with her mother. They were both members of the Communist Party of Germany.
