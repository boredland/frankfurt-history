# NSDAP local group und NSDAP Frauenschaft Nordost

*Arnsburger Straße 46*

![Photo of the former offices of the NSDAP local group and NSDAP Women's League Nordost](../../images/a539e7ec756388d67f9e591670880568_original.jpg)
*2019 — Author: Martin Starl | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2019-693 | Description: Photo of the former offices of the NSDAP local group and NSDAP Women's League Nordost*

This was the location of the local Bockenheim branch of the National Socialist German Workers’ Party (NSDAP) and the associated National Socialist Women’s League. The local branches were subordinate to the district level. Each branch was responsible for some 1,500 households, and oversaw the cell and block leaders in their areas. A block leader was responsible for 40 to 60 households, which he kept watch over and spied on. The local branches not only gathered information on party members in their neighbourhoods, but also everyone living there. In this way, social control extended even into individual residential buildings. In Frankfurt, records show the city had 47 branch leaders, 670 cell leaders and 3,630 block leaders.
