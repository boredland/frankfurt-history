# Stolen Place

*Landgrafenstraße 12*

![Landgrafenstraße 12](../../images/36e5f2d1894adbaa4e184d201a8ff563_original.jpg)
*1907 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Landgrafenstraße 12*

The property at Landgrafenstraße 12 originally belonged to Johanna Rothschild. She was persecuted for anti-Semitism and had to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939. Later, the food and egg wholesaler Josef Münstermann & Co. ran a camp for civilian workers here.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
