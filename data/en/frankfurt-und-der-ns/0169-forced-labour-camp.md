# forced labour camp

*Alt Praunheim 42-44*

![Forced labor camp, Alt Praunheim 42-44](../../images/b0c78315b61ec6b5cf9cdec7799621f0_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-24209 | Description: Forced labor camp, Alt Praunheim 42-44*

In 2003, by way of extensive research, the Institut für Stadtgeschichte (Institute of Municipal History) was able to prove that this was the site of a forced labour camp that lodged workers from Western and Eastern Europe. These persons performed forced labour for different companies, including the Günther & Co. precision tool factory, the J. & C. A. Schneider shoe factories, and possibly also the Dr. Hans Witteler hardware and metalware factory of Frankfurt.
