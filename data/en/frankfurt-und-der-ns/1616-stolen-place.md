# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 114*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 114](../../images/85db96a7ecb848d0bcf2a298c5b71da6_original.jpg)
*Author: Hedwig Loy | License: HMF | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 114*

With a purchase contract dated December 14, 1938, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 3,439 square meters from Sophie Stavenhagen for the price of 80,000 Reichsmark. The property was intended for a future expansion of the Palmengarten and had previously been used for residential purposes.
