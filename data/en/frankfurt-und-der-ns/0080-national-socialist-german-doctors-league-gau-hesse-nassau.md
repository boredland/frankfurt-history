# National Socialist German Doctors’ League, “Gau” Hesse-Nassau

*Gutleutstraße 8-14*

![Photo of the headquarters of the Nazi leadership of the Hesse-Nassau region](../../images/cbe9d58f9c9a8db7e223576aaa0067be_original.jpg)
*Author: Hannah Reeck | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Z Nr. 1933-321 | Description: Photo of the headquarters of the Nazi leadership of the Hesse-Nassau region*

The Nazi leadership of the Hesse-Nassau region had their headquarters here from 1933 to 1940. The building, constructed in 1906, was originally designed as a hotel. Renamed “Adolf Hitler House”, this also became the official residence of Jakob Sprenger, the regional leader of Hesse-Nassau. In 1940, Sprenger and his staff moved to Horst-Wessel-Platz (now Rathenauplatz) in the city centre.
