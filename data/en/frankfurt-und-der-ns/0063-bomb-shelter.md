# Bomb shelter

*Zeilweg (ohne Nummer)*

![Photo of a bomb shelter located in Zeilweg](../../images/f1a227c7432217ca163db48f639b4d9d_original.jpg)
*1998 — Author: unbekannt | License: Denkmalamt Stadt Frankfurt | Description: Photo of a bomb shelter located in Zeilweg*

This bomb shelter was on the factory grounds of the VDM-Halbzeugwerke GmbH, a subsidiary of the Vereinigte Deutsche Metallwerke AG, in Heddernheim. The company, which was essential to the armament effort, made use of forced labourers. Construction work lasted from March to June 1944. It was carried out by the Hochtief AG company, which had also planned the facility. By this point in time, building was actually prohibited, but the VDM was granted an exemption. The shelter offered protection for 4,000 persons on six floors. It was torn down before 1984.

Construction company: Hochtief AG
 Architect: unknown
