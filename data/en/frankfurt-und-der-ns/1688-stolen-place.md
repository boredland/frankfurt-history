# Stolen Place

*Junghofstraße 14*

![Junghofstraße 14](../../images/7ac02b3d750331e566f8b329c1f41160_original.jpg)
*1930 — Author: Willy Keim | License: ISG | Description: Junghofstraße 14*

The property at Junghofstraße 14 had originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
