# forced labour camp

*Alexanderstraße 69*

![Picture postcard with general view of the Weilwerke (torpedo works)](../../images/64a43b222b0b23c29e4eba5ae5db98a2_original.jpg)
*unknown — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, Sammlung Church, C80403 | Description: Picture postcard with general view of the Weilwerke (torpedo works)*

The Torpedo-Werke manufactured office machines and bicycles. During World War II the company operated four forced labour camps in Frankfurt. One of them was located here on the factory grounds. In 2003, the Institut für Stadtgeschichte (Institute of Municipal History) was able to prove that, in 1943, 22 men from Lithuania and 11 women from Russia were exploited here. Other sources indicate that 400 forced labourers were lodged here.
