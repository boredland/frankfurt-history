# Stolen Place

*Eschenheimer Anlage 33-34*

![Eschenheimer Anlage 33-34](../../images/9a5a346138f209f17edfec06f3915b3a_original.jpg)
*1937 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Eschenheimer Anlage 33-34*

With a purchase contract dated May 10, 1937, the city acquired the 2,173 square meter, developed property from the company Baruch Strauss o.H.G, which belonged to Jewish owners, for the price of 40,000 Reichsmark. The city sold the property to Deutsche Städtereklame before the end of the war.
