# Stolen Place

*Wolfsgangstraße 22b*

![Wolfsgangstraße 22b](../../images/8d4b4112ed2ae5e07c4839ad235c422d_original.jpg)
*ca. 1930 — Author: Justus Helme | License: ISG, Frankfurt Best. S8-1 Nr. 4054 | Description: Wolfsgangstraße 22b*

The property at Wolfsgangstraße 22b originally belonged to Charlotte W. and Gertrud R. The anti-Semitic persecutees had to transfer their house to their children as a gift in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
