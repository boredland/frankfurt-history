# forced labour camp

*Alt Praunheim 13*

![Forced labor camp, Alt Praunheim 13](../../images/c95be6c394247897288f8b8f84e180ad_original.jpg)
*1944 — Author: J. van Emden | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Z Nr. 2002-65 | Description: Forced labor camp, Alt Praunheim 13*

From 1943 onwards, this was the location of a forced labour camp probably operated by the Witteler builder’s hardware and metalware factory. In 1943, 16 persons from Lithuania were registered here. The Günther & Co. precision tool factory based at Voltastrasse 41 also lodged forced labourers here.
