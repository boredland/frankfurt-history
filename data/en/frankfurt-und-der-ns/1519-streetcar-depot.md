# Streetcar depot

*Schwabstraße 18*

![](../../images/c34f439a494ac5467c20db9488329e7e_original.jpg)

It is said that some of the streetcar drivers were against the National Socialists. Slogans against the NSDAP and Hitler were written on the walls of the streetcar depot toilets or leaflets were displayed. Streetcar drivers were persecuted by the Gestapo (Secret State Police) and imprisoned for denunciation, i.e. betrayal by colleagues.
