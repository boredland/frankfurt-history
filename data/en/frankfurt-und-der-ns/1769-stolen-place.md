# Stolen Place

*Stettenstraße 32*

![Stettenstraße 32](../../images/3a6ba5e11047b98f65e38cdd594f7145_original.jpg)
*1933 — Author: Heinrich Jourdan | License: ISG | Description: Stettenstraße 32*

The property at Stettenstraße 32 originally belonged to Dr. Karl Heller. The anti-Semitic persecutee's house was foreclosed on in 1938.
