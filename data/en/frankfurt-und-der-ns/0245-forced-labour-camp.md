# forced labour camp

*Leipziger Straße 22*

![Forced labor camp, Leipziger Straße 22](../../images/543560a660f22e6fa8ea25db6c876d5d_original.jpg)
*1905 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-20124 | Description: Forced labor camp, Leipziger Straße 22*

The Betten Zellekens bed and upholstered furniture company had its headquarters at Leipziger Strasse 22. A few doors down the street, at Leipziger Strasse 17, it operated a camp that lodged 4 forced labourers from Eastern Europe, 2 men and 2 women.
