# Stolen Place

*Steinweg 8*

![Steinweg 8](../../images/42d6fa2fa279a0470b76aa4837e8a4ea_original.jpg)
*ca. 1885 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Steinweg 8*

The property at Steinweg 8 originally belonged to a Jewish owner. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer during the Nazi era.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
