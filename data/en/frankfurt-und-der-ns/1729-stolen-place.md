# Stolen Place

*Mauerweg 36*

![Mauerweg 36](../../images/b7f352a65e4b5e6d5ee235fdf1143fe0_original.jpg)
*ca. 1905 — Author: Carl Diehl | License: ISG | Description: Mauerweg 36*

The property at Mauerweg 35 had originally belonged to Julius Neumann. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
