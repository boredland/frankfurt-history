# Bomb shelter

*Gutleutstraße 231*

![Photo of a bomb shelter located in Gutleutstraße](../../images/7632a448fe9c19b0df98ea64dfe3c581_original.jpg)
*1965 — Author: unbekannt | License: Denkmalamt Stadt Frankfurt | Description: Photo of a bomb shelter located in Gutleutstraße*

This was the site of a round bomb shelter tower that offered protection to the employees of the electricity works during air raids. It was the designer Leo Winkel who had the idea for this type of bomb shelter, for which reason it was also known as a “Winkel tower”. The tip of the round tower was to deflect the bombs. This shelter was torn down in 1978.

Construction company: unknown
 Architect: Leo Winkel
