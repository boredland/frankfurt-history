# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 102*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 102](../../images/31518a533772d3b06ce67adde79da27f_original.jpg)
*License: ISG | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 102*

With a purchase agreement dated August 5, 1937, the city acquired the undeveloped property of 2,399 square meters from Dr. phil. A. Sondheimer. The purchase price was 85,000 Reichsmark. The property was to be used as a Reichsarboretum.
