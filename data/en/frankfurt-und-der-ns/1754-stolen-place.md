# Stolen Place

*Schaumainkai 55*

![Schaumainkai 55](../../images/6d2851c52e7da2bb1c7ae485f9a36b65_original.jpg)
*1935 — Author: Hannah Reeck | License: ISG | Description: Schaumainkai 55*

With a contract dated August 15, 1940, the city acquired the developed property at Schaumainkai 55 / corner of Städelstraße 28 with an area of 3,322 square meters from the Jewish couple Paul Oppenheimer and his wife Gabriele, née Errera, for the price of 115,000 Reichsmark. The property was intended for residential purposes.
