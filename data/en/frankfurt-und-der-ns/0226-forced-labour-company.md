# forced labour company

*Hanauer Landstraße 475*

![Forced labor operation, Hanauer Landstraße 475](../../images/de1a03c6eb2ab175cc94a133e62021d9_original.jpg)
*1970 — Author: Hans Schleiss | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2012-247 | Description: Forced labor operation, Hanauer Landstraße 475*

The Meuser & Co. machine tools factory made use of forced labourers from 1942 onwards. They were lodged in a camp at Pfortenstrasse 55. Other sources come to the assumption that the company also had a camp on its factory grounds at Hanauer Landstrasse 314. Some 280 workers from Western and Eastern Europe are thought to have been housed here in barracks.
