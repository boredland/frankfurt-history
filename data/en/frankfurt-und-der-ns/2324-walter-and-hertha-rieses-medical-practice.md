# Walter and Hertha Riese's medical practice

*Kronberger Straße 5*

![Today, there is a post-war building at Kronberger Straße 5.](../../images/330d38c551c76aa4b76c712f8d083c48_original.jpg)
*2025 — Author: Jasmin Klotz | License: gemeinfrei | Description: Today, there is a post-war building at Kronberger Straße 5.*

Walther and Hertha Riese, a married couple, ran a joint doctor’s office in Frankfurt. They were both psychiatrists, and Hertha was also a women’s rights activist.

Around 1930, following the recommendation of physician and sexologist Magnus Hirschfeld, Hertha Wind, a trans woman, consulted with Walther and Hertha Riese. The couple not only enabled Hertha Wind to have hormone treatment but also initiated contact with the surgeon Max Flesch-Thebesius, who later operated on her in Sachsenhausen Hospital.
