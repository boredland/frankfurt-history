# Confessing Congregations Office

*Schillerstraße*

![Placeholder image](../../images/Hier%20fehlt_dfdc6d631f271e842082099187aa5623_original.jpg)
*License: HMF*

Among other activities, the office of the Confessing congregations of the Evangelical Church organized help for Frankfurt members of the Confessing Church. The secretary Mrs Suchanek hid money collected during offertory for persecuted members of the Church in her handbag. This was a safe hiding place, because the Gestapo appeared frequently and confiscated the money she kept in the office safe.
