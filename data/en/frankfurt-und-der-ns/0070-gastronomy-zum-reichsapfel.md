# Gastronomy "Zum Reichsapfel"

*Große Friedberger Straße 39*

![](../../images/43d6fa3835966bc11279a733b41b3346_original.jpg)
*Author: © Katharina Müller, VG Bild-Kunst, Bonn 2022*

The members of the National Socialist German Workers’ Party (NSDAP) regularly met in the “Zum Reichsapfel” restaurant and inn. Here, the NSDAP district and local branch leaders planned joint actions on the evening of the boycott of Jewish-owned shops and businesses, companies, legal offices and medical practices (1 April 1933). In the run-up to the meeting, local branch leaders had to prepare lists with possible targets.
