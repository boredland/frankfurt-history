# Hitler Youth hostel

*Schaumainkai 53*

This was the location of the Villa de Neufville. The owners, persecuted due to their Jewish heritage, had to sell their residence to the city of Frankfurt in mid-1937. Initially, the city administration planned to house one of the municipal museums here. When that idea was shelved, the Hitler Youth took over the building. They converted it and removed the decorative stucco throughout the interior. With its twelve large rooms, this became one of twenty Hitler Youth hostels in Frankfurt. The organisation had planned to establish a total of 80 hostels in the city, though they attracted negative attention when they wrecked the hostel building at Schaumainkai 53.
