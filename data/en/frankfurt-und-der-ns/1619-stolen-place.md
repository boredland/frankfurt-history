# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 136-138*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 136-138](../../images/84de466369acdac0f1b4682fe896648c_original.jpg)
*License: HMF | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 136-138*

Johann Wilhelm Lehr designed a modern administrative building in the New Building style for the publishing house of the social democratic daily newspaper "Volksstimme". The building, which was occupied by Union-Druckerei- und Verlagsanstalt GmbH in 1929, was occupied by armed SA troops on May 5, 1933 and vandalized. Five days later, the Gestapo confiscated the property, which later became the property of the Prussian state and fell victim to a bomb hit during the war.
