# Stolen Place

*Kaiserstraße 70*

![Kaiserstraße 70](../../images/4c2e5ee14e0bfaad19c621a03c6aed32_original.jpg)
*1946 — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Kaiserstraße 70*

The property at Kaiserstrasse 70 had originally belonged to Gustav Droller, Siegmund May, Moritz Löwenberg and Louis and Julius Landauer. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939. The building authority rejected several purchase contracts. The last purchase contract was signed in 1941.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
