# Stolen Place

*Große Friedberger Straße 24*

![Große Friedberger Straße 24](../../images/75a9c4de21264b73204aac7d3681eb99_original.jpg)
*1936 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Große Friedberger Straße 24*

With a purchase contract dated November 18, 1937, the city acquired the developed property at Große Friedberger Straße 24 with 107 square meters from the company Grünebaum & Haas in liquidation, which had Jewish owners. The purchase price was 25,000 Reichsmark. The city wanted to use the area for the so-called "Altstadtgesundung" (Old Town Rehabilitation), which envisaged gutting and new construction.
