# forced labour camp

*Flughafen*

![Forced labor camp, airport](../../images/5505d438d6f4dd81ff9181dfe8869624_original.jpg)
*1936 — Author: Paul Wolff | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7Wo Nr. 3831 | Description: Forced labor camp, airport*

The fire brigade of the Frankfurt airport operated a camp for forced labourers on the new airport grounds. It is not known who was forced to work here. The Hinzert concentration camp outside of Trier, however, operated a subcamp here towards the end of the war. Between November 1944 and February 1945, 100 inmates were lodged in barracks. They were assigned to clean-up work following aerial attacks.
