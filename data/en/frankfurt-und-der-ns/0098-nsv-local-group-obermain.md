# NSV local group Obermain

*Danziger Platz 2*

![Picture postcard showing the building at Danziger Platz 2, headquarter of the local Obermain chap­ters of the Natio­nal Socialist People’s Welfare, the Natio­nal Socialist German Workers’ Party and the Natio­nal Socialist Women’s League](../../images/2fa8f59dac8ea3e0c2d92a0084f503e4_original.jpg)
*n.D. — Author: unbekannt | License: HMF / Sammlung Church C80397 | Description: Picture postcard showing the building at Danziger Platz 2, headquarter of the local Obermain chap­ters of the Natio­nal Socialist People’s Welfare, the Natio­nal Socialist German Workers’ Party and the Natio­nal Socialist Women’s League*

The local Obermain chapter of the National Socialist People’s Welfare, the second largest mass organization of the Nazi period, had its headquarters here. Among other activities, the People’s Welfare organized the Winter Relief Organization, which formally, however, was an independent entity. It offered its services only to members of the so-called people’s community; others in need were denied its support. It even regarded the homeless and persons who relied on begging as competition for its own efforts to collect donations. These persons were considered “asocial” and persecuted as such. During the war, the organization provided relief to refugees and persons who had been bombed out of their homes. To that end it also acquired the furniture and clothing of Jews who had been deported or had fled the country, and was involved in the auctions of goods thus stolen.
