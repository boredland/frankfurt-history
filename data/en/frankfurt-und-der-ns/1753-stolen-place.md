# Stolen Place

*Schäfergasse 9*

![Schäfergasse 9](../../images/71b1b3d380b56c50edd0fbc32d7bb38c_original.jpg)
*1884 — Author: Wehe-Wehl | License: HMF | Description: Schäfergasse 9*

The property at Schäfergasse 9 originally belonged to Jakob Rohr and his wife Auguste, née Rosenzweig. The anti-Semitic persecutees had to cede the usufruct of their house to a non-Jew in 1938.
