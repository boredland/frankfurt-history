# forced labour camp

*Buchrainstraße 18*

![Forced labor camp, Buchrainstraße 18](../../images/dd738be9322cc0a9a2f88a62aa68b6ee_original.jpg)
*2002 — Author: Claus Graubner | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7C Nr. 2015-913 | Description: Forced labor camp, Buchrainstraße 18*

The Peter Görres welding equipment manufacturer made use of forced labourers who were lodged in a camp in Gruneliusstrasse. There are indications that forced labourers lived on the factory grounds in Buchrainstrasse as well. In 1946, the police reported that 1 civilian worker from France was registered there from 19 January 1944 to January 1945. This can be inferred from a document in the holdings of the Arolsen Archives.
