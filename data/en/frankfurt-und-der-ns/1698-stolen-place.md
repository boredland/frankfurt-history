# Stolen Place

*Kennedyallee 115-117*

![Kennedyallee 115-117](../../images/366b560fa55dcdbaa8ed25b2d4d77beb_original.jpg)
*1939 — Author: Georg Schmidter | License: ISG | Description: Kennedyallee 115-117*

With a purchase agreement dated June 2, 1939, the city acquired the developed property with an area of 1,760 square meters from the widow Hedwig Sichel-Beer for the price of 60,000 Reichsmark. The property was to be used for institutional purposes.
