# Stolen Place

*Fahrgasse 94*

![Fahrgasse 94](../../images/73f73b85392d2b4a66547f3044f4de10_original.jpg)
*1935 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Fahrgasse 94*

The property at Fahrgasse 94 originally belonged to a Jewish owner. Due to persecution, he had to sell his house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.
