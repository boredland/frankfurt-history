# Stolen Place

*Seeheimer Straße 8*

![Seeheimer Straße 8](../../images/b703d90eeca04c873d886e3695b953de_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Seeheimer Straße 8*

The property at Seeheimer Strasse 8 originally belonged to Emilie Hirsch, née Heinemann. The anti-Semitic persecutee was forced to sell her house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
