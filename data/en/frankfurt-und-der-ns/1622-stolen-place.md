# Stolen Place

*Börsenstraße 17*

![Börsenstraße 17](../../images/20e91742cbc857ca5fa37392bd047dc1_original.jpg)
*1910 — License: ISG | Description: Börsenstraße 17*

The property at Börsenstraße 17 originally belonged to a Jewish owner. Due to persecution, he had to sell his property to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.
