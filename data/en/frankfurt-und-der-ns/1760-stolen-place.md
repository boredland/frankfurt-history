# Stolen Place

*Schumannstraße 56*

![Schumannstraße 56](../../images/ec5ec698eff22191d0b23b787947e9f7_original.jpg)
*ca. 1900 — Author: Günther & Seeger | License: ISG | Description: Schumannstraße 56*

The property at Schumannstraße 56 originally belonged to Richard Karl Ullmann as well as Dr. Karl Ullmann and his wife Flora, née Desenberg. The anti-Semitic persecutee Richard Karl Ullmann transferred his co-ownership share in the property to his non-Jewish mother-in-law by way of a gift in 1939. Dr. Karl and Flora Ullmann sold their co-ownership share to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
