# Stolen Place

*Westendstraße 58*

![Westendstraße 58](../../images/7235ea142e6f191f5c6cd2984b8ec7f3_original.jpg)
*1939 — Author: Herzog | License: ISG | Description: Westendstraße 58*

The property at Westendstraße 58 originally belonged to Julius Jablonski and his wife Elsamaria, née Hofmann. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
