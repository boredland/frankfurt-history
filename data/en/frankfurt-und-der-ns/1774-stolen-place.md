# Stolen Place

*Waldfriedstraße*

![Waldfriedstraße](../../images/2d56f5ba1ab16bf1375314dc76b2a22d_original.jpg)
*ca. 1905 — Author: Friedrich Lauffer | License: HMF | Description: Waldfriedstraße*

In a purchase agreement dated December 7, 1938, the city acquired the developed villa property with an area of 175,722 square meters from Dr. Carl von Weinberg for the price of 335,000 Reichsmark. An additional purchase price of 750,000 Reichsmark was agreed for the art collection in the Villa Waldfried. The property was intended for school and institute purposes.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
