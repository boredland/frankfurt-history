# forced labour camp

*Friedensstraße 9*

![Picture postcard with Kaiserplatz, Friedensstraße and Kaiserstraße](../../images/2235017e704959f960d9393355651425_original.jpg)
*around 1900 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF, Sammlung Church, C80370 | Description: Picture postcard with Kaiserplatz, Friedensstraße and Kaiserstraße*

The Alfred Teves machine and fixtures factory made use of several hundred forced labourers in its plants in Bonames and Fechenheim. In addition to civilian workers, these persons also included prisoners of war. They were lodged in several camps, one of which was located here at Friedensstrasse 9 in Kaffee Messing.
