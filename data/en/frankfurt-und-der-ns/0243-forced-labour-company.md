# forced labour company

*Lahnstraße 60*

![Forced labor company, Lahnstraße 60](../../images/ef84134cf98d09ba93673eb5a96addd6_original.jpg)
*1914 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-29221 | Description: Forced labor company, Lahnstraße 60*

Under National Socialism, the Bünte & Remmler light fixture factory profited from forced labour. According to the Institut für Stadtgeschichte (Institute of Municipal History), 51 forced labourers from Russia, 4 from France, and 129 from Germany worked here in 1943. The majority of them were women. No information about the location of their lodgings has yet been found.
