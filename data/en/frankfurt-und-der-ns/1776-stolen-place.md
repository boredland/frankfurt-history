# Stolen Place

*Weserstraße 46*

![Weserstraße 46](../../images/188438fc86a8a7bc0dcb9bd68970205d_original.jpg)
*before 1926 — Author: Kurt Reich | License: ISG | Description: Weserstraße 46*

Founded in 1908, the Israelitische Mädchenheim e.V. was located at Taunusplatz 17 and offered 54 places. It had to cease operations during the Nazi era. The property passed into non-Jewish ownership.
