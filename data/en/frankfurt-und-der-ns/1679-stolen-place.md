# Stolen Place

*Heddernheimer Landstraße 32*

![Heddernheimer Landstraße 32](../../images/6086a56d7b14979bcb754fdeb6fc30dd_original.jpg)
*2008 — Author: Wolfgang Kamberg | License: ISG | Description: Heddernheimer Landstraße 32*

The property at Heddernheimer Landstraße 32 had originally belonged to Sigmund Simon and his wife Jeanette, called Jenny, née Oppenheimer. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer and his wife in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
