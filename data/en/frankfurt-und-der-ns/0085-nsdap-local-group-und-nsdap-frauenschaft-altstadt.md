# NSDAP local group und NSDAP Frauenschaft Altstadt

*Buchgasse 11a*

![Photo of the offices of the NSDAP local group and NSDAP Women's League Altstadt](../../images/042f8c17e764e770fdf6ded195947976_original.jpg)
*1910 — Author: unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S7A Nr. 1998-2427 | Description: Photo of the offices of the NSDAP local group and NSDAP Women's League Altstadt*

A glimpse inside the 1935 directory shows that altogether five local chapters of National Socialist organizations had their headquarters here: the NS German Workers’ Party, the NS Women’s League, the NS People’s Welfare, the NS Factory Cell Organization, and the NS Crafts, Trade, and Industry Organization.
