# Stolen Place

*Bockenheimer Landstraße 8*

![Bockenheimer Landstraße 8](../../images/4874086c41d6601ccc815f20e11a72ad_original.jpg)
*ca. 1935 — License: ISG | Description: Bockenheimer Landstraße 8*

The city acquired the developed property from Baron M. von Rothschild in a purchase agreement dated June 11, 1937. The purchase price amounted to 190,000 Reichsmark. The property was intended for the Plastics Institute.
