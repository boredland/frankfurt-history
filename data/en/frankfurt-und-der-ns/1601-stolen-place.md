# Stolen Place

*Alte Gasse 69*

![Alte Gasse 69](../../images/9cd495234a820cc6966c94863749553b_original.jpg)
*ca. 1878 — Author: Friedrich August Doermer | License: HMF | Description: Alte Gasse 69*

The property at Alte Gasse 69 originally belonged to the animal agent Julius Isaac Sichel, Klara Buchheim, née Sichel, the minor Margot Mina Strauss and the widow Henriette Sichel, née Neumann. Due to persecution, the Jewish owners had to sell the property with its inn, store and five apartments on a plot of 82 square meters to a non-Jewish innkeeper for 20,000 Reichsmark in a purchase contract dated 13 August 1938. The officially tax-assessed value amounted to 25,900 Reichsmark. a compensatory levy to the German Reich was not set.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
