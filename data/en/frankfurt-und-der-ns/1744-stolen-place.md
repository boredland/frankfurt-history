# Stolen Place

*Ostendstraße 26*

![Ostendstraße 26](../../images/24cc1d66af7a02db37b137a6b2edaefc_original.jpg)
*n. d. — Author: Postkartensammlung Church | License: HMF | Description: Ostendstraße 26*

The property at Ostendstraße 26 had originally belonged to Salomon Strauss and his wife Hedwig, née Strauss. The anti-Semitic persecutees were forced to sell their house to a non-Jewish buyer in 1939.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
