# forced labour camp

*Forsthaus/Gasthaus Biegwald wohl Biegweg*

![Picture postcard with the Biegwald inn](../../images/19614a48854fd7de7c424553d42cae90_original.jpg)
*before 1936 — Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte, Frankfurt Best. S17 Nr. 899-6 | Description: Picture postcard with the Biegwald inn*

The Biegwald guesthouse and forester’s lodge was already housing 36 Polish forced labourers as far back as September 1942. In 1943, 110 Soviet prisoners of war and 30 Polish forced labourers had their accommodations here. They were put to work at VDO Tachometer, which had its factory at Königstrasse 103 (now Gräfstrasse).
