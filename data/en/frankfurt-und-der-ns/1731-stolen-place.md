# Stolen Place

*Mendelssohnstraße 78*

![Mendelssohnstraße 78](../../images/9094ab4837e31858a4be391406445970_original.jpg)
*1930 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Mendelssohnstraße 78*

The property at Joseph-Haydn-Straße 78 (now Mendelssohnstraße) originally belonged to Florence Flersheim, née Livingston. The anti-Semitic persecutee had to sell her property to the German Red Cross, Frankfurt district office, in 1938.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
