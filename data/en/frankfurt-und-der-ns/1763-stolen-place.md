# Stolen Place

*Seilerstraße 10*

![Seilerstraße 10](../../images/ec9fbdf7a2bade2fce81d42f4839fc6c_original.jpg)
*ca. 1860 — Author: unbekannt | License: ISG | Description: Seilerstraße 10*

With a purchase agreement dated November 30, 1938, the city acquired the developed property at Seilerstraße 10 with an area of 1,303 square meters from F. Beifuss, who had been persecuted for anti-Semitic reasons, for the price of 25,000 Reichsmarks. The property was intended for office purposes.

Research: Jutta Zwilling
