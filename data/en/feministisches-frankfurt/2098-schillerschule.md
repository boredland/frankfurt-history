# Schillerschule

*Emilie Braach - Education for girls*

Emilie Braach - Bildung für Mädchen

![Fotografie Schillerschule](../../images/19121c091e4cd51484ad5f631036e661_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Schillerschule/Gemeinfrei | Description: Fotografie Schillerschule*

Founded in 1908, the Schillerschule was the first high school for girls in Frankfurt and enabled young women to obtain their high school diploma. Emilie “Mile” Braach, who later became a journalist and writer, attended the Schillerschule from 1909 onwards. During the Nazi era, she was no longer allowed to publish her work as a so-called “jüdische Mischling ersten Grades"" (first-degree Jewish half-breed) and secretly wrote fictional letters to her daughter in exile. After the war, she became involved as a contemporary witness.
