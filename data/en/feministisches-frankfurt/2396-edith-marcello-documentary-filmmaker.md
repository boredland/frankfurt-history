# Edith Marcello - documentary filmmaker

*Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V.*

"Edith Marcello (née Schmidt, *1937) is a documentary filmmaker. She studied painting and film, and her work engages with themes such as labor migration, the women’s movement, labor struggles, and new social movements. She directed around 80 documentary film productions, primarily for television. She is particularly known for the films she made together with David Wittenberg, such as Pierburg: Ihr Kampf ist unser Kampf (1974/75). These films document wildcat strikes and factory occupations in Germany and France, capturing the social upheavals of the 1970s.

In 1975/76, she created the film Das hat mich sehr verändert – Das Frankfurter Frauenzentrum with a women’s group from the Eckenheim Women’s Center in Frankfurt. The film served the women in their own educational and informational work and inspired newly emerging women’s groups. She lives in Frankfurt am Main."
