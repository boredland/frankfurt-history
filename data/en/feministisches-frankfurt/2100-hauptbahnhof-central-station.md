# Hauptbahnhof (Central Station)

*Anna Edinger – Departure for the Peace Congress*

Anna Edinger - Abfahrt zum Friedenskongress

![Blick auf den Hauptbahnhof](../../images/a5ba1dfc8e6eae3620e950b0a8ac7adf_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF | Description: Blick auf den Hauptbahnhof*

Frankfurt Hauptbahnhof (Central Station), opened in 1888 as the Centralbahnhof, has been a place of coming and going – including for Anna Edinger. From here, she set off on her journey to the nternationalen Frauenfriedenskongress (International Women's Peace Congress) in Den Haag in 1915. She was one of the few activists in the German women's movement who courageously fought for peace and against the First World War. Anna Edinger supported many social projects, was active in the women's movement, and was a significant benefactor of Frankfurter Universität (Frankfurt University).
