# Rosemarie Trockel:  Frankfurter Engel (1994)

*Memorial to the Persecution and Criminalization of Homosexuals*

![Das Mahnmal für die Verfolgung und Kriminalisierung von Homosexuellen, entworfen von Rosemarie Trockel, steht seit 1994 auf dem Klaus-Mann-Platz in der Frankfurter Innenstadt.](../../images/1402d0cd469aae12f89cdf0d8f3e030f_original.jpg)
*Author: Axel Schneider | License: IMH / Axel Schneider – www.frankfurter-engel.de | Description: Das Mahnmal für die Verfolgung und Kriminalisierung von Homosexuellen, entworfen von Rosemarie Trockel, steht seit 1994 auf dem Klaus-Mann-Platz in der Frankfurter Innenstadt.*

Rosemarie Trockel became known in the 1980s as an artist determined to break with traditional gender roles once and for all. In her artworks, she took the stovetop – a clichéd symbol associated with the (house)wife – and humorously transformed it into an abstract form. Her serial wall installations with repeating circular motifs only faintly suggest their original kitchen reference.

In 1992, her design won the sculpture competition organized by the Initiative Mahnmal Homosexuellenverfolgung e.V. (IMH, Memorial to the Persecution of Homosexuals Initiative). Rosemarie Trockel’s Frankfurter Engel (Frankfurt Angel) was installed in 1994 as the first fully three-dimensional monument of its kind to commemorate the persecution of queer people under National Socialism.

After many years of dedicated work, the IMH dissolved in 2025. Further information about the Frankfurter Engel can be found in the theme area Frankfurt und der NS (Frankfurt and National Socialism).

*Author: Naomi Rado, Kulturamt Frankfurt, 2025*
