# Home – Gerbermühle

*Marianne von Willemer – Literary circle on the Main*

Wohnhaus - Gerbermühle

![The Gerbermühle at Frankfurt am Main](../../images/e5a509ffdfdbb17ba4e6427c97496f19_original.jpg)
*Author: Ludwig von Roeßler | License: HMF/Gemeinfrei | Description: The Gerbermühle at Frankfurt am Main*

The Gerbermühle on the eastern edge of Frankfurt has a long history. From 1785 onwards, Johann Jakob Willemer (1760–1838) used it as a summer residence after leasing the mill property and converting it into a residence. His wife Marianne von Willemer (1784-1860) turned the Gerbermühle into a lively meeting place for her circle of cultural friends. In the summer of 1814, Johann Wolfgang von Goethe (1749–1832) also came to visit. A close, inspiring relationship developed between him and Marianne.

*Author: Sofia Schnitzler*
