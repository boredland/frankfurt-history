# Modesalon- Friedensstraße 2

*Toni Schiesser - Atelier und Verkaufsraum*

![Modeatelier Toni Schiesser in der Friedensstraße 2, Fotografie](../../images/216d8c99b5535bd821758ac6651eee85_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF | Description: Modeatelier Toni Schiesser in der Friedensstraße 2, Fotografie*

Toni Schiesser's fashion salon was located at Friedensstraße 2 from 1951 onwards. Its central location near Theaterplatz, now Willy-Brandt-Platz, made the studio easily accessible – it remained there until it closed in 1998. The salon produced tailor-made designs that were in line with international trends and appealed to a wealthy urban clientele.

Without formal training, Toni Schiesser worked her way up to become a sought-after designer through perseverance and entrepreneurial skill. During the Nazi regime, she benefited from the repossession of Jewish businesses by purchasing high-quality fabrics there at low prices. She exploited the exclusion and persecution of others for her own advantage and looked away when humanity was needed.

*Author: Sofia Schnitzler*
