# Gestapo Headquarters on Lindenstraße

*Irene Block – Imprisonment under National Socialism*

Irene Block - Inhaftierung im NS

![Fotografie des Gebäudes der Lindenstraße 27, bis März 1945 Sitz der Geheimen Staatspolizei](../../images/e1b257e7917f5e00310b769cfaee21c5_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Institut für Stadtgeschichte | Description: Fotografie des Gebäudes der Lindenstraße 27, bis März 1945 Sitz der Geheimen Staatspolizei*

From 1940 to 1945, the Geheime Staatspolizei - Gestapo (Secret State Police) had its headquarters at Lindenstraße 27. The basement of the building contained a prison where numerous people were detained and mistreated. The building was originally constructed in 1896/97 as a convent. Irene Block, a lawyer and tax advisor, was interrogated there several times and spent three days in Gestapo prison. She helped many Jewish clients with their emigration procedures at her law office. Even after she had helped them obtain exit visas, she continued to assist them with their travel documents.
