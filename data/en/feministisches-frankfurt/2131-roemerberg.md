# Römerberg

*Lore Wolf – Witness to the Bücherverbrennung (book burning)*

Lore Wolf - Zeugin der Bücherverbrennung

![Bücherverbrennungen auf dem Römerberg 10.05.1933, Öl auf Leinwand](../../images/b3c13499a23ad4ff8ca7c5717eaa0574_original.jpg)
*Author: Heinz Woelcke | License: HMF | Description: Bücherverbrennungen auf dem Römerberg 10.05.1933, Öl auf Leinwand*

As in many German cities, books were burned on the Römerberg in Frankfurt on May 10, 1933. A large pyre was erected and books were brought in by cart – mainly Marxist works and literature that did not fit into the National Socialists' worldview.

For Lore Wolf (1900 - 1996), this event was a defining moment. She worked in the Frankfurt city administration and became involved in the trade union movement at an early age. In the 1930s, she was active in the resistance against National Socialism and fled to France, where she joined the Resistance. After her arrest, she spent several years in prison. After 1945, she continued her political activism and supported victims of Nazi persecution in Frankfurt.
