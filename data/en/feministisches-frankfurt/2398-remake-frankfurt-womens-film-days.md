# Remake. Frankfurt Women's Film Days

*Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V.*

![](../../images/77e97dcd031b02d7f5bedb83a6de66ac_original.jpg)

In 2018, the Kinothek Asta Nielsen launched Remake. Frankfurter Frauen Film Tage*. Its aim is to present film works by women*, bringing them back to the screen in new constellations and contexts and inspiring contemporary audiences with these screenings.
Remake has no competition program and sets no requirements regarding genre, format, or premieres. The festival’s work is accompanied by restoration projects and publications. It focuses on themes such as women, emancipation, queer cinema, and alternative perspectives on society, addressing phenomena such as migration, colonialism, and racism.
The festival takes place at the Pupille – Kino in der Uni e.V., located in the Studierendenhaus at Goethe University Frankfurt.
