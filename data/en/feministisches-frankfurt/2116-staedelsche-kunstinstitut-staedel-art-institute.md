# Städelsche Kunstinstitut (Städel Art Institute)

*Ottilie W. Roederstein – Artistic success at the Städel*

Ottilie W. Roederstein – Kunsterfolg am Städel

![Ottilie W. Roederstein und Elisabeth Winterhalter](../../images/1640322a2eea7d22d7f934166dcb8f69_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF/Privatbesitz | Description: Ottilie W. Roederstein und Elisabeth Winterhalter*

The Städelsche Kunstinstitut (Städel Art Institute), known today primarily as the Städel Museum with its attached art college, is located directly on the Main River. Johann Friedrich Städel, banker and philanthropist, laid the foundation for a public collection and education in his will in 1816. In 1893, painter Ottilie W. Roederstein rented a studio at the Städel Art Institute and taught her students there. Ottilie W. Roederstein was an internationally successful artist who held her own in a male-dominated art world. Together with her partner, the physician Elisabeth Winterhalter, she lived and worked in an equal partnership—an exceptionally self-determined life for that time.
