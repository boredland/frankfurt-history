# Vegetarian restaurant

*Anna Beyer – Cooking in the resistance*

Anna Beyer - Kochen im Widerstand

![Anna Beyer als Küchenhilfe in einer vegetarischen Gaststätte in London 1943, Fotografie](../../images/5e4047a97015a252c0edcad06025cfd4_original.jpg)
*Author: Unbekannt | License: Hessisches Hauptstaatsarchiv Wiesbaden/Unklar | Description: Anna Beyer als Küchenhilfe in einer vegetarischen Gaststätte in London 1943, Fotografie*

Anna Beyer opened the vegetarian restaurant “Vega” at Steinweg 10 in 1936. She was inspired to do so by a similar project run by the Internationalen Sozialistischen Kampfbunds - ISK (International Socialist Fighting League) in Cologne. The restaurant helped to finance and network the resistance against the Nazi regime. Anna Beyer grew up in a social democratic family and was politically active from an early age. After years in exile in Paris and London, she returned to Frankfurt in 1945 and became involved in politics and the reconstruction of the city.
