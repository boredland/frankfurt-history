# “Therapeutikum” – Hochstraße 8

*Else Wüst – Working in the Resistance*

Else Wüst - Arbeiten im Widerstand

![Die Hochstraße mit Bockenheimer Tor](../../images/d7159f8b07747db0a7054dc1da2d2134_original.jpg)
*Author: Carl Friedrich May | License: HMF/Gemeinfrei | Description: Die Hochstraße mit Bockenheimer Tor*

Else Wüst (1892-1974) opened her own “Therapeutikum” at Hochstraße 8 in 1938. During the Nazi regime, she offered shelter there to an elderly Jewish couple and two families. As a social democrat, social worker, and member of the Frankfurt Quakers, she was secretly involved in the resistance against the Nazi regime: she helped with Kindertransports, organized departures, and supported people who were under threat.
