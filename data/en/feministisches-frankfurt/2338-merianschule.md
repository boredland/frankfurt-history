# Merianschule

*Maria Sibylla Merian – Butterfly Researcher*

![Merian school](../../images/436f3234d636065a3aec3f647f46e190_original.jpg)
*2025 — Author: Sofia Schnitzler | License: HMF/Gemeinfrei | Description: Merian school*

The Merian School at Burgstraße 21–23 opened in 1886. The school building was destroyed during the Nazi regime and by a bomb; teachers and children were evacuated to the countryside. After 1945, classes were temporarily held at the Glauburg School. Around 1960, the Merian School reopened its doors in the rebuilt premises.

The Merian School commemorates the Merian family of artists.
Maria Sibylla Merian was a Frankfurt artist and naturalist who studied butterflies and their development for decades. With her detailed observations and watercolors, she contributed significantly to the understanding of metamorphosis and created important pictorial documentation of natural history.
