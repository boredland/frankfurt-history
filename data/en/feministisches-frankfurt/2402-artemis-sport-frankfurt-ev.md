# Artemis Sport Frankfurt e.V

![Logo Artemis Sport Frankfurt e.V](../../images/a33d2bf3d7025aaac7260e61d9a80496_original.png)
*Unknown — Author: Unbekannt | License: LIBS e.V. | Description: Logo Artemis Sport Frankfurt e.V*

Once a year, during the CSD weekends, the supporting programme for the CSD demonstration takes place in the middle of Konstablerwache. The Artemis Sport Frankfurt drinks stand is an important meeting point every year. Eva Hartard and Gisela Weil were no longer there in 2000 when the first Artemis drinks stand was launched, but they can tell us all about the founding days of Artemis Sport Frankfurt in the 1980s.
