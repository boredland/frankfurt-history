# Elisabeth Winterhalter & Ottilie W. Roederstein

*Scheffelstraße 2-14*

![Ottilie W. Roederstein and Elisabeth H. Winterhalter](../../images/5b38edc8f643b494715902008806b32e_original.jpg)

In front of Maingau Hospital, we hear Dagmar Priepke talk about two remarkable women who, individually and as a couple, made a difference in Frankfurt and always championed women's rights, especially women's educational opportunities.
