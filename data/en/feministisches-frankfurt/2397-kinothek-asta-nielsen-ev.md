# Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V.

![Film still from DIE FILMPRIMADONNA (2013, director: Urban Gad) starring Asta Nielsen in the lead role (right in the picture).](../../images/d30ad1fa957c8423e15f067ccfdfbd15_original.png)
*1913 — Author: Urban Gad | License: Eye Filmmuseum Amsterdam | Description: Film still from DIE FILMPRIMADONNA (2013, director: Urban Gad) starring Asta Nielsen in the lead role (right in the picture).*

The Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V. has been active since 1999 with film presentations, thematic programs, retrospectives, and showcases that draw attention to the often overlooked film work of women and queer cinema culture. It also promotes critical engagement with gender relations in film. Since 2018, it has organized Remake. Frankfurter Frauen Film Tage* every two years.

The Kinothek collects archival material related to its thematic focus areas and actively uses it for its film programs. The archive is continuously expanding and includes a Super 8 home-movie collection, video and 16mm film holdings. In addition, it comprises a library, a paper archive with grey literature such as program flyers, posters, and catalogues, as well as a photo collection and estates related to feminist and queer filmmaking.

The archive is open to visitors every Friday from 11 a.m. to 2 p.m
