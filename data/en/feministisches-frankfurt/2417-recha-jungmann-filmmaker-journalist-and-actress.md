# Recha Jungmann – filmmaker, journalist and actress

*Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V.*

![Portrait Recha Jungmann](../../images/46f7025c90b0b8c4ae521a2ce33ebace_original.jpg)
*1995 — Author: Heiko Arendt | License: Heiko Arendt | Description: Portrait Recha Jungmann*

Recha Jungmann (*1940 in Bad Kreuznach) studied music and theatre at the Hochschule in Hanover and worked, among other engagements, as an actress at the Schauspiel Frankfurt. She completed a second degree at the Hochschule für Gestaltung Ulm (Film Department). As a writer and director, she worked for ZDF and produced numerous television documentaries, including the three-part series Unsere Mütter, Unsere Väter (1981/1982).

She also produced three feature films: Two Right, Two Left, Drop One (1972), Etwas tut weh (1979), and Zwischen Mond und Sonne (1980/1981), as well as two short films that were shown at international festivals. Her films explore the consequences and impacts of the Nazi era on family dynamics, intergenerational questions, and gender relations. Recha Jungmann lives in Frankfurt.

*Author: Kinothek Asta Nielsen e.V.*
