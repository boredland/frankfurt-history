# Kunstkabinett

*Hanna Bekker vom Rath – Gallery Owner*

![Postkarte mit Blick in die Kaiserstrasse Richtung Innenstadt](../../images/c5ed3e3d0b8b921d83486aed6fa2d18c_original.jpg)
*1907 — Author: Frankfurter Lichtdruckanstalt | License: HMF | Description: Postkarte mit Blick in die Kaiserstrasse Richtung Innenstadt*

In 1947, Hanna Bekker vom Rath opened the Frankfurter Kunstkabinett at Kaiserstrasse 5. There she presented works by artists who had been marginalized during the Nazi regime. In the early years, the gallery generated little income, but she consistently used it to promote art. As a painter and mediator, she used exhibitions, acquisitions, and trips abroad to promote artistic positions that had long been suppressed.
