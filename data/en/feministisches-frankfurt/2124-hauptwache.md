# Hauptwache

*Annett Stoltze – Participation in the Revolution*

Annett Stoltze - Beteiligung an der Revolution

![Blick auf die Hauptwache mit Wachparade, Katharinenkirche und die Zeil](../../images/1c5eb638e110c7a0c0ce7950e1d5fc2c_original.jpg)
*Author: Johann Jakob Tanner | License: HMF/Gemeinfrei | Description: Blick auf die Hauptwache mit Wachparade, Katharinenkirche und die Zeil*

The Hauptwache is a former guardhouse that was built in 1671 and gave its name to the town square of the same name. Today, it is home to a central S-Bahn and U-Bahn station. Soldiers were stationed at the Hauptwache, which also housed a prison. During the so-called Frankfurt Wachensturm in 1833, around 100 rebels attempted to storm the Hauptwache and Konstablerwache in order to free political prisoners – a significant event in the Vormärz movement. Anna Margaretha Stoltze (1813-1840) was probably aware of the plans. She courageously stood up for prisoners, smuggled messages, helped with escape attempts, and was convicted and socially ostracized for her actions.
