# Ironmongery

*Anna Elisabeth Zahn – Management of the craft business*

Anna E. Zahn – Leitung des Handwerksbetriebs

![Platzhalter](../../images/ee1966f7f56103ae1baeb63686d7c114_original.jpg)
*Author: Hannah Rödel | License: HMF/Hannah Rödel | Description: Platzhalter*

In 1713, Anna Elisabeth Zahn (1669-1757) took over the ironmongery at Töngesgasse 72 after her husband's death. As a widow, she was allowed to continue running the craft business – a rare opportunity for women to work in a guild system that otherwise excluded them. She trained journeymen, signed commercial letters, and ran the business as a “master” with a seal.
