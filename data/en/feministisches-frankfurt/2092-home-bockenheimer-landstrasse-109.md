# Home– Bockenheimer Landstraße 109

*Jenny Apolant – Legal advice for women*

![Fragebogen Auskunftstelle der Gemeindeämter der Frauen, Frankfurt am Main Bockenheimerlandstraße 109](../../images/8168329ced3c7f635390c6055a8fc685_original.jpg)
*1908 — Author: Unbekannt | License: HMF | Description: Fragebogen Auskunftstelle der Gemeindeämter der Frauen, Frankfurt am Main Bockenheimerlandstraße 109*

The “Auskunftsstelle für die Gemeindeämter der Frau"" (Information Center for Women's Municipal Offices) was located at Bockenheimer Landstraße 109, set up in Jenny Apolant's private apartment. The Allgemeine Deutsche Frauenverein - ADF (General German Women's Association) had founded this center in 1905 under the direction of Helene Lange. Its task was to document and raise awareness of the social position of women at the municipal level.

Jenny Apolant initially worked here alone. She reviewed documents, collected statistics, and organized information from various social areas. This work marked the beginning of her commitment to women's politics and was an important step in her later career as a local politician and women's rights activist.
