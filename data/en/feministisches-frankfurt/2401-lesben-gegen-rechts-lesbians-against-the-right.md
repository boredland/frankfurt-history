# Lesben gegen Rechts (Lesbians against the right)

*Klaus-Mann-Platz*

![](../../images/ac8b35659194e73475ae2d07152979f2_original.jpg)

The memorial to the persecution of homosexuals is located on Klaus-Mann-Platz. Here, Inanna and Maren from the regional group Lesben gegen Rechts Rhein-Main (Lesbians Against the Right Rhine-Main) talk about their important work.
