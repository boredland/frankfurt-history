# SCHLAU Frankfurt

*A queer civic education and anti-discrimination project*

![SCHLAU information booth at CSD Frankfurt 2024](../../images/b4b9817ef02df280f9c08fe902fea611_original.jpg)
*2024 — Author: SCHLAU Frankfurt | License: SCHLAU Frankfurt-gemeinfrei | Description: SCHLAU information booth at CSD Frankfurt 2024*

Queer stories belong in schools – SCHLAU Frankfurt

SCHLAU which originally started out as SchLAu (Gay and Lesbian Education) is now a queer civic education and anti-discrimination project that addresses not only sexual diversity but also romantic and gender diversity. In workshops with teenagers and young adults from Year 8 onwards, participants have the opportunity to talk to queer people – our volunteer team members. We provide a low-threshold introduction to the broad topic of queer lifestyles. We invite young people to reflect on social prejudices and ask any questions they may have about queerness: When and how did you realise you were queer? How did your parents react, your friends? Which discriminatory experiences have you had – and how do you deal with them? We cannot answer all of them; for example, we are not a sex education project.
